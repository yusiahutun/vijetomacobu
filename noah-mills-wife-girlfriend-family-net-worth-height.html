Does Noah Mills Have A Wife Or A Girlfriend?

CBS’s NCIS: Hawai’i might be one of the best shows that aired in 2021. And even better were its casts who dedicated themselves to investigating crimes on the water. Noah Mills, a member of the team (portraying Jesse Boone), was one of the fans’ favorites.

But today we aren’t discussing his on-screen life. Rather, let’s take the time to learn all about his relationship.

Does Noah Mills Have A Wife Or A Girlfriend?

As of September 2021, Noah Mills didn’t have a wife and appeared to be single. Rumors had that he was still recovering from a bad breakup with his Canadian girlfriend then.

Noah is a Toronto, Ontario, Canada-born actor who moved to the US while he was just a kid. He was then raised in Baltimore, Maryland for a while until his family returned to their hometown. Not soon after their return, Noah fell in love with a girl from his boarding school on Victoria Island, and they enjoyed dating bliss for quite a while.

The NCIS star was still admittedly a “kind of unorganized and crazy” guy back then. But one day, he was approached by a local scout who offered him a modeling job. For someone who grew up camping and loved adventures, this struck him as an unmissable opportunity. 

Unfortunately, the start of his career was coupled with a bad break-up with his girlfriend. Thus, Noah packed up his bags and left for New York.

Interestingly, Noah remained private about his dating life then after.

Noah Mills Family

Noah Mills comes from a Canadian family. Both of his parents are Canadian, who has spent a part of their lives in America.

As for his siblings, Noah is the youngest of five children. He has two brothers and two sisters whose identities were kept private. However, we do know that his older brother Mike Brennan is a police officer who was there during the 9/11 tragedy.

Reportedly, Noah’s maternal grandparents Ralph Langstaff Crow, Jr. and  Barbara Godwin, and his parental grandparents Edward Ensign Mills and Julie Chapman, all were from New York. So, if you did the roots, Noah’s ancestors were originally American.

Before 2021, Noah resided in Los Angeles, California.

Noah Mills Net Worth

Noah Mills garnered a net worth of under $3 million by 2021.

An actor by profession, Noah first started his career as a model. He has started in innumerable fashion advertisements, including Salvatore Ferragamo, Dolce and Gabbana, Tommy Hilfiger, Michael Kors, and Tom Ford. Also, he has worked with stars like Steven Meisel, Peter Lindbergh, Steven Klein, Mario Testino, Mikael Jansson, Craig Mcdean, and Mert & Marcus.

It was 2010 when Noah kickstarted his movie journey appearing as Samantha Jones love interest, Kim Cattrall in the box-office hit Sex and the City 2. Since then, he has graced over 15 moves and TV shows including 2 Broke Girls (2011), The Brave (2017), The Enemy Within (2019), and The Falcon and the Winter Soldier (2021)

As of 2021, Noah was represented by Gersh and Luber Roklin Entertainment. Previously, he was also signed to IMG Models.

So, what does Noah spends his money on? Well, it’s on house and aesthetics. He was one of the most successful models around who built his dream house in Venice Beach, LA from scratch. His 950 square foot bungalow flaunted extraordinarily ceiling heights, wooden frames, a detached garage, and outdoors dilled with lush green trees and hedges.

In light of his education, Noah graduated from the University of British Columbia, Vancouver, in psychology.

When Noah isn’t working, he enjoys writing, producing, acting, and surfing. 

His Athletic Line

As said before, Noah is into aesthetics, and not only house decors, the actor has also invested in a clothing line named NM Athletics. His company was launched in early December 2019, after purchasing a 5,000 square foot lot.

NM Athletics featured a range of athletics-friendly pieces that were meant for “pre, during, and post workouts.” Talking about his product, Noah said, “it’s the right fit, the right weight, and a no-fuss non-designer style.”

Facts About Noah Mills

Noah Mills was born on April 26, 1983. That made him 38 years of age when  NCIS: Hawai’i premiered in 2021. Corresponding to his birthday, Noah is of the Taurus zodiac.

Yes, Noah is present over Instagram @noahmills, and Facebook @noahmillsfansite, where he flaunted 122K and 10K followers respectively. Also, find him over Twitter @Noah_live with 19.6K followers, as of September 2021. Most of his posts then were about his home aesthetics and his modeling pictures.

Noah Mills stands tall at a height of 6 feet 2 inches (187 cm) and has 11 US/10.5 US/45 Eu shoe size. Mentioning his distinct features, Noah has brown eyes, dark hair, and a long triangular face.
