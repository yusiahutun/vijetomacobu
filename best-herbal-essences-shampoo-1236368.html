    How to Use Herbal Essences Shampoo

    If you're wondering what's the best way to use a Herbal Essences shampoo, take a look at our quick and easy guide below:

  • Pour warm water all over your hair ensuring that it's entirely wet.
  • Plop a quarter-sized dollop of your preferred Herbal Essences shampoo in your hands and lather it up well.
  • Use your fingertips to gently massage it into your scalp – work the lather through the rest of the hair.
  • Again, use warm water to thoroughly rinse out any shampoo residue that may be left over.
  • If you use a conditioner, then apply it to the lengths of your hair, avoiding the scalp area. Rinse it out after a minute or so.
  • Use a towel to dry your hair and avoid rubbing as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Style your hair the way you like it – you're good to go!

    • Keep in mind that you need to check the bottle for specific instructions – this way you can make the most out of your Herbal Essences hair product.
