About

American Rapper M.C. Breed was born Eric Tyrone Breed on 12th June, 1971 in Flint, Michigan, USA and passed away on 22nd Nov 2008 Ypsilanti, Michigan, USA aged 37. He is most remembered for his singles "Ain't No Future in Yo' Frontin'", which peaked at #66 on the Billboard Hot 100, and "Gotta Get Mine" (featuring 2Pac), that made it to number 6 on the Hot Rap Singles.
