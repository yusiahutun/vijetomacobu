5 Interesting things about her.

Not many people know this, but Nicki Minaj is gorgeous without makeup. Below we take a look at other interesting things about the rapper.

It is safe to say the hip-hop world missed Nicki Minaj during her hiatus from making music. The rap star had announced her retirement in 2019 to focus on raising her son Papa Bear who she welcomed in 2020 with her husband, Kenneth Petty. 

However, the “Chun Li” crooner soon returned to the music scene in 2021. She released the song “Seeing Green,” featuring Drake and Lil Wayne, and a mixtape “Beam Me Up, Scotty.” 

After her return, Minaj explained on Twitter that the break helped put her in a better frame of mind. She also revealed it provided her with enough time to bond with her son. The rap star disclosed that her son hated rap music while she was pregnant. Instead, the kid only responded to soothing/classical music. 

Minaj also touched on the loss of her father, who died after a car struck him in a hit-and-run incident. She confessed that losing her father was the most devastating loss she had ever faced.

However, 2022 has started in a much better fashion for Minaj. This year alone, she has released four new songs. The song “We Go Up,” a collaboration with artist Fivio Foreign follows the songs “Bussin” and “Do We Have a Problem,” both featuring Lil Baby. 

There is also “Blick Blick” with Coi Leray. It is good to have the queen of rap back releasing steady music. So let’s look at five other exciting things about Minaj in that spirit.

Nicki Minaj Looks Beautiful Without Makeup

So many artists are defined by a specific look, costume, or outlook, and Nicki Minaj is one of such artists. The rapper came into the music scene, dazzling fans with her colorful costumes and hair. She also had a reputation for donning different looks in pictures and videos. 

Minaj is often heavily doused in makeup to lift and enhance her beauty.  However, there have also been occasions where the rapper appeared with little to no makeup. Yet, the Trinidad-born beauty has looked naturally beautiful, and it proves that Nicki Minaj, without makeup, would still slay. 

In 2014 Minaj left her hotel with no makeup to rehearse for the “Philly 4th of July Jam and Grand Fireworks Finale. The rapper who donned a red tracksuit looked gorgeous. Minaj also once arrived in Paris, France, for a Karl Lagerfeld photo shoot without hardly any makeup on.

Another time she went bare was a time she was spotted walking through the JFK airport terminal. Minaj, who wore a fur coat over her shoulder and a cheetah print ankle boots coupled with a huge high heel, wore no makeup. 

Nicki Minaj Starred In “Ice Age 4”

Like many Hollywood artists, Minaj has also tried her hand at acting apart from music. In 2012 the rapper starred in the film “Ice  Age: Continental Drift.” In the movie, Minaj voiced a Woolly Mammoth called Steffie.

After its release, the movie became Minaj’s first number one box film, although it is not in her credits. The movie also became the second-highest-grossing film and the highest-grossing animated film.

The film also starred celebrities like Jennifer Lopez, who voiced Shira, and rapper Drake who voiced Ethan. However, Minaj revealed she said only about five or six lines in the movie.

Nicki Minaj Announced Her Pregnancy Wearing a Bikini

Much like everything else she does, Minaj announced her pregnancy in style in 2020. The rapper took over the internet when she posted pictures of herself cradling her baby bump on Instagram.

Donning yellow hair in one picture and blue hair in another, Minaj wore a bikini adorned with big and colorful embellishments. Minaj captioned the picture “preggers,” and fans and colleagues, and other celebrities flooded her comments with congratulatory messages. She also had a fringe on the bottoms in one of the pictures.

Nicki Minaj Lives In a $10 Million Mansion.

Living in a multimillion-dollar mansion is one of the things celebrities do, and Nicki Minaj is no exception. One of the richest in the industry, the rapper lives in a $10 million mansion with her husband, Kenneth Petty, and her son Papa Bear.

The house, located in the heart of Los Angeles, features six bedrooms and eight bathrooms. The colossal property sits on 7000 square feet of land. Minaj often shows off some parts of her house in most of her pictures.

In a particular photo, she posed in her kitchen, which showed off a large display of China wares.The kitchen also has wooden floors and a white panel lining the lower half of the wall. Her living room is also exquisitely designed. It features high ceilings and a floor-to-top window overlooking the garden.

Nick Minaj Got Berated By Malcolm X’s Daughter 

In 2014 Minaj released an iconic picture of Malcolm X looking out a window with a semi-automatic weapon to promote her song “Lookin Ass.” As soon as it dropped, the rapper got reprimanded by fans who felt it was inappropriate.

Malcolm X’s daughter Ilyasah Shabazz and the family attorney L. Londell McMillan quickly released a statement to rebuke Minaj for her actions. 

Ilyasah disclosed that the family did not endorse Minaj’s use of the picture. The artwork did not depict what her late father stood for or his legacy. Afterward, Minaj apologized and then removed the artwork from her social media. She also explained that the single’s cover was never the official artwork. 

Meanwhile, in his statement, McMillian warned people to desist from using the picture on social media. He further disclosed that failure to do so would result in legal actions taken against them.
