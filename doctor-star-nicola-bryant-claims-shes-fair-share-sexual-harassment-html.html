Doctor Who starNicola Bryant claims she's had her 'fair share' of sexual harassment

Doctor Who star Nicola Bryant has claimed she's faced her 'fair share' of sexual harassment.

The actress, 62, who played Peri Brown on the iconic sci-fi show from 1984 to 1986, claimed she was pestered by TV bosses to 'sleep with them' to secure another acting role, and it has carried on well into the latter stages of her career.

Nicola, who has gone onto star in My Family and Holby City, also admitted that she has turned down acting roles in the past that would require her to have sex with television bosses.

Candid: Doctor Who star Nicola Bryant has claimed she's faced her 'fair share' of sexual harassment (pictured with Colin Baker on the show) There is no suggestion that Colin was involved in the harassment

Speaking on the My Time Capsule podcast, Nicola explained that such harassment from TV bosses took a huge toll on her mental health.

'I've had it in all different shapes and forms. I'm 62, you'd think this nonsense would end and it's still going on.

'They take you to dinner and say, ''I can offer you a reality show and two sitcoms if you sleep with me.'' Like a banking deal.

'There was a director. We were on a train and he said his flat was just one minute away, would I take the script for his next job.

'We walk round the corner and he pounced. He goes, ''This is your job, it's a massive role, but you've got to sleep with me.'' No. I ran out and I've never spoken to him since.'

Nicola went onto claim that one showbusiness figure has even flashed their private parts at her.

Tough time: Nicola, who has gone onto star in My Family and Holby City, also admitted that she has turned down acting roles in the past that would require her to have sex with bosses

She added: 'I turn around and he's got something out and he's put it on his desk. I'm like, ''Really?''

Going onto reveal she's rejected some roles in the past that required her to have sex, she added: 'It turned into a conditional job. I am so tired of it. I'm like, ''When does it end? When I'm 70? When I'm 80?''

'It's been in every layer of this industry.'

During her stint on Doctor Who, Nicola appeared alongside Peter Davison and Colin Baker in their incarnations of the titular role. They are not accused of any wrongdoing.

An American botany major, she decided to join The Doctor on his travels when he defeated The Master, and witnessed his regeneration after he sacrificed herself to save her from spectrox poisoning.

In her final appearance, The Doctor was led to believe that Peri was killed by an arthropod-like creature named Kiv, when in reality she married King Yrcanos of Thoros Alpha.
