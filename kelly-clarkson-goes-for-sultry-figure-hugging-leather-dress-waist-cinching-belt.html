Kelly Clarkson goes for sultry in figure-hugging leather dress and waist-cinching belt amid exciting

The Kelly Clarkson Show host made her talk show comeback last week

    Kelly Clarkson is finally back to hosting her eponymous daytime talk show, and with that, her fabulous outfits have also made a comeback!

    The "Since U Been Gone" singer premiered the fifth season of her show last week, and brought back the same welcoming energy, star-studded guest list, and her style, all from her brand new studios in New York City's Rockefeller Center.

    Now, ready for a second week from her new home, the star is kicking off the week with a bang in the fashion department.

    For Monday's installment of the show – which featured comedian Nikki Glaser and singer Darius Rucker as guests – Kelly opted for a sultry leather look.

    The host looked stunning in a button-down, black leather maxi dress, which she paired with one of her signature wide, waist-cinching belts, accessorizing with gold chain necklaces and she styled her hair pin straight.

    For Kelly's premiere last week, she was welcomed by her new NBC neighbors Lester Holt, Al Roker, Savannah Guthrie and Hoda Kotb with open arms, and used the episode as an opportunity to honor New York City's doormen and women, who made up the audience.

    After an introduction from Andy Cohen, she started her show with: "I love you, I'm so excited to be here," and continued: "It is so great to finally be back on the air… We get to do our show again."

    She added: "And from our new home, this is such an iconic building, 30 Rockefeller Center," and noted: "I can't believe I got to not only move here but work from here. It is such a blessing."

    "I also want to take a moment to salute the slew of people here at 30 Rock who hustled around the clock to get our studio ready for today," she continued, adding: "This legendary space used to be home to David Letterman and Conan O'Brian back in those days… The effort for you to see what you're seeing today was nothing short of a miracle."
