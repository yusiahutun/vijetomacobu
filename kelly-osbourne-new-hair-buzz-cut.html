Kelly Osbourne shows off new buzz cut

    Kelly Osbourne has shocked fans with a dramatic new hair cut.The star took to Twitter to share a photo revealing a shaved section of her hair on one side, with the rest of her short locks dishevelled.For the snap, she wore matching lilac lips and heavy winged eyeliner.

    Some of her 3.65million followers asked Kelly whether the shot had been played with, prompting Kelly to deny any doctoring. "That was not photoshopped or filtered just really good lighting!" she wrote.

    Kelly is known for being vocal on the social media network. Just last week, she tweeted her annoyance at media outlets reporting that she had gained weight.

    The daughter of Sharon and Ozzy is known to have battled her weight in the past but appears to be in shape and happy since splitting from fiancé Matthew Mosshart.

    Kelly is currently the face of healthy snacks Wheat Thins Popped.

    Despite being on TV since she was a teenager, the star still had to get used to seeing her face enlarges on adverts across the Big Apple.

    She captioned one Instagram image: “I can't believe that almost half of the billboards in#TimeSquare are pictures of me I feel like #Liza thank you #poppedwheatthins! “Don't forget to keep tweeting with the hash tag #poppedwheatthins we have a few more balloons to pop!!!! Help me pop pop pop.”

    "When did I all of a sudden gain 20lbs?" she posted alongside a defiant picture. "I just asked my ass and she can't seem to remember either! #MustBeASlowNewsWeek!"The picture shows her sporting a lime green jumper and skirt. While the picture doesn't show the Fashion Police presenter's face , she can be seen holding her top up to reveal her stomach.
