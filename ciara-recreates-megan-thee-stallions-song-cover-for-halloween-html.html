Ciara Recreates Megan Thee Stallion's Song Cover for Halloween

Music star Ciara decided to pay homage to Megan Thee Stallion for Halloween, and both artists’ fans can’t get enough. 

The “Goodies” singer channeled her inner “hot girl” on Saturday, October 31, 2020. The mother of three’s Halloween look came after she also dressed up as Cardi B with her son by her side. Megan and Cardi collaborated on a song, “WAP,” in August 2020. 

Ciara recreated Megan Thee Stallion’s cover art for ‘Girls in the Hood’ 

Megan Thee Stallion isn’t a stranger to a stunning album or song covers. Since she catapulted to fame in 2019, the rapper has allured her fans with her sultry looks and accessories. Ciara shared a photo on her Instagram page of herself recreating Megan’s cover from her song, “Girls in the Hood.” Ciara wore a pink bandana-printed top with matching bottoms and a pink fur hat. She also added Megan’s “hot girl” gold hoop earrings to the costume. In a separate video, Ciara also danced to Megan’s song with Nicki Minaj, “Hot Girl Summer.” 

After she posted her photos of her costume, Ciara received many likes and comments from her fans. Many of them were impressed with how Ciara showed off several of Megan’s characteristics, like her buzzed-about knees. 

“Yassss!!! You got them good, Megan knees as well, I see,” one commenter said. 

“Ciara, this is why I love you!!! Real recognize Real!!!” another fan exclaimed.

“Cici Thee Stallion!!!!” another user said. 

“You just won Halloween 2020,” a fourth fan chimed in. 

Ciara dressed up as Cardi B for Halloween in addition to Megan Thee Stallion

Although Ciara’s rendition of Megan turned multiple heads, she got a head start on her Halloween looks on Friday, October 30, 2020. She posted several photos of herself as Cardi B. Ciara decided to pay homage to the “Money” rapper by recreating Cardi’s cover from her 2018 album,  Invasion of Privacy.  She wore a yellow wig and a black and white jacket with sunglasses.

Ciara’s costume enlisted the help of her oldest son, Future Zahir Wilburn. Future dressed up as Offset and danced with his mom in one of Ciara’s Instagram slides. The singer finished the look with acrylic nails with Kamala Harris and Joe Biden and encouraged her fans to vote in the 2020 presidential election. 

Ciara received numerous likes and commented about her Halloween costume of Cardi as well shortly after posting. 

“OMGGGG SO GOOOOOODDDD,” one fan wrote. 

“I thought it was the real Cardi,” another chimed in. 

“STOP. This is ICONIC,” a commenter said. 

Ciara’s relationship with Megan Thee Stallion and Cardi B

Ciara’s decision to pay homage to both Megan and Cardi comes after showing she’s a fan of them both. In November 2019, Ciara and Megan seemingly developed a friendship and shared their fun times on social media. The artists posted a video of themselves having a “twerk off” in an empty driveway while playing Ciara’s song, “Melanin.” 

Ciara supported Cardi throughout her career. Cardi took notice of Ciara’s Halloween costume and shared her thoughts about it on Instagram. 
“’I’m gagging! I love it! I’m so hype! I’ve always been a huge fan of @ciara and became an even bigger fan after I met her! She is the most sweetest and the most nicest person in the world! A true peach! Staying sweet and humble was the prayer!” Cardi said of Ciara’s costume.
