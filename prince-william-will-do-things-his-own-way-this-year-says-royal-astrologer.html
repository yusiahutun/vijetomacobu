Prince William will 'do things his own way' this year, says royal astrologer

Prince William will “make a difference” and “do things his own way” in 2024, according to an astrology reading.

Prince William is set to have a productive year in 2024 according to the stars, and it will be “game-changing” for him. There were also predictions about his relationship with his wife Kate Middleton. The next 12 months are looking to be very positive for William indeed after a few turbulent years within The Firm such as difficulties with his brother, Prince Harry. Harry and his wife, Meghan Markle, left the royal family in January 2020. They did so, apparently, without warning and so it may have caused the Prince of Wales some stress and uncertainty, but now four years later, things seem to be looking up.

Prince William’s astrology reading for 2024

A royal astrologer, Debbie Frank, told HELLO! Prince William would have a “game-changing” year ahead, according to the reading.

He will apparently start the first couple months of 2024 in “planning mode” as Saturn “schedules his responsibilities and commitments.” Then, from March 25 until April 25, there will be a full moon that will affect both him and Kate, 41. It will see the couple come together in a “team effort.”

In May, he will have a “feel-good factor” in both his personal life and public engagements.

William’s 42nd birthday is important

According to his 2024 astrology reading, Prince William’s birthday (June 21) is an important one as he will “break with tradition” and “create new initiatives.”

Frank explained: “The great mover and shaker planet Uranus encourages breaking with tradition and creating new initiatives. We can expect William will ‘make a difference’ and do things his way during this time.

She continued: “Autumn is another big pulse point for the Prince. The eclipsed New Moon on his Mars in statesman-like Libra expands his reach in the world. October is going to be a power month for the Prince and Princess of Wales. The autumn and winter ready William for 2025 which continues to raise his star.”

A change of heart for William

Despite the drama of Megxit, Prince William may not mind standing out from the rest of his family and doing things differently if his 2024 astrology reading is to be correct. This is also a change in attitude from 20 years ago, when the future king apparently “worried” over public reaction to his university decision.

While studying at the University of St Andrews in Scotland – where he began in 2001 – he changed his major from Art History to Geography after things didn’t “click” for him, but it wasn’t without much thought, royal authors Dylan Howard with Andy Tillett claim their book Royals At War.

“William had been immersing himself in the world of Old Masters and classical art—he hadn’t clicked with the subject, and worse, he was terrified of public failure,” they penned. “He was preyed upon by the prospect of getting a reputation for weak work or an uninspired degree at the end of years of hassle and fuss for everyone.”

They added: “Given William’s status and, yes, the huge amount of work that had gone into creating a safe space for him to work and live, Charles counseled caution.”
