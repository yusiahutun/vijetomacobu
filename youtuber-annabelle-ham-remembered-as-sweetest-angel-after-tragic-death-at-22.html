YouTuber Annabelle Ham remembered as 'sweetest angel' after tragic death at 22

YouTube lifestyle star Annabelle Ham has been remembered as the “sweetest angel” after the tragic news of her death at the age of 22. Her sisters confirmed her passing and her family posted a statement on her Instagram profile.

The tragic news of the aspiring influencer’s passing was confirmed by her family on social media. Annabelle ran a YouTube channel where she shared her experience as a student in Georgia and regularly filmed shopping hauls.

YouTuber Annabelle Ham has died at 22

Annabelle passed away after an “epileptic event”, her family confirmed in a devastating statement on her Instagram on Tuesday, July 18.

“Annabelle experienced an epileptic event and has gone on to the gates of heaven,” they wrote alongside a family selfie with Annabelle’s sisters. “She struggled with this for a long time and wanted to raise awareness for it, which we will do in honor of her.”

Her family remembered her as “beautiful and inspiring” and someone who “lived life to the fullest”. “Everyone she met was inspired by her energy and the light inside her soul that shined so [brightly],” the tribute added. “She was, and will forever be, so so loved.”

Tributes paid to Annabelle

The Instagram post was filled with poignant tributes from Annabelle’s fans and YouTube subscribers who shared their fond memories of the aspiring influencer.

“She was the sweetest angel,” one tribute read. “Miss you forever Annabelle. Sending so much love and prayers.”

“Praying for all of you so hard,” another person said. “Annabelle reminded all of us how important it is to make the people you love smile & feel loved. She will be so missed.”

Another subscriber added: “In shock right now. I’ve watched Annabelle since she was in high school and it felt like watching an older sister. Prayers for you all.”

Annabelle Ham was a bright YouTube star

Annabelle successfully grew her YouTube channel since launching it in 2014. She documented her life as a college student in Georgia and posted vlogs, her makeup routine, and daily life.

She shared lengthy videos from her trips to Florida, California, and Hawaii and uploaded Christmas vlogs.

Annabelle gained 77,000 subscribers on YouTube and had a similar fan base on Instagram, where she posted daily outfits and pictures with her friends.
