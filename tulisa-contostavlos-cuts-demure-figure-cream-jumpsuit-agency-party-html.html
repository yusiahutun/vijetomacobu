Tulisa Contostavlos cuts demure figure in cream jumpsuit at agency party

She is currently on bail after crashing her £150,000 Ferrari in an alleged drink driving accident.

The 27-year-old former X Factor judge swapped her usual seductive look for a more demure and sophisticated ensemble, donning a chic cream jumpsuit as she partied with the likes of Myleene Klass and Jamelia.
