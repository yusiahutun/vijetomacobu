Toast Hand Warmers Are Going to Be Your Newest Winter Obsession

If you’ve gone outside at all within the past few days, then you know it’s a bit chilly out there. But life goes on, which means we still have to get out of our warm and cozy beds and go to work — even when we don’t feel like it.

While that may entail bundling up in scarves, jackets, mittens, socks, and whatever else you can find to keep yourself warm on the way to and from work, it doesn’t have to be quite that miserable. Instead of looking like a mismatched marshmallow, why not invest in a cute pair of wireless Toast Handwarmers and have a little more fun staying “toasty”?

Toast Wireless Handwarmers

We’re sure you’re a little confused; we were too, at first. But, we promise these little mitts are totally worth checking out. 

(Photo Credit: Amazon)

Created by Smoko — maker of some of the most adorable products we’ve ever seen — these unique little handwarmers ($42.99, Amazon) are squishy, soft, and wireless! All you have to do is charge the battery pack before you leave and then go about your day with (literally) toasty hands. They’re even designed with adjustable straps and customized heat settings, so you can make them as snug and as warm as you’d like. Use them during your commute, at the office, or in the comfort of your own home at night — they’re an instant cure-all for the chilly days of winter (and they’re good for a smile, too). 

Smoko Plush S’mores Heated Slippers

(Photo Credit: Amazon)

If you’re more of a heated slippers kind of person, then Smoko also sells an adorable pair of S’mores-themed heated slippers ($37, Amazon). Unlike the handwarmers, these slippers are powered via USB, so you’ll need to be near an outlet or USB port while wearing them. But that just gives us another reason to snuggle up on the couch! 

Smoko also sells a lot of other fun items, like The Unicorn Cookbook and Glitter Wine Glasses. If you need us, we’ll be bundled up, decked out in our S’mores slippers and our toast mitts, scrolling through the Smoko website.
