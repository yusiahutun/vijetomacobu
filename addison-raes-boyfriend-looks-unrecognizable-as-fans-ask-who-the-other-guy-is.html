Addison Rae's boyfriend looks 'unrecognizable' as fans ask who the 'other guy' is

Addison Rae’s latest TikTok has fans confused, as her boyfriend, Omer Fedi, has undergone a major hair transformation. So much so, that some fans are wondering who the ‘other guy’ is. Omer, who is an Israeli record producer was previously known for his long red locks, although it seems they’ve been well and truly ditched.

As one of the most followed people on TikTok, fans have been invested in Addison Rae‘s life since she burst onto the scene in 2019 when she posted dancing videos on the platform. Since then, the star has grown tremendously in popularity, even becoming friends with the Kardashians, especially Kourtney.

Addison Rae’s boyfriend Omer Fedi undergoes ‘hair transformation’

Taking to her 88.6 million TikTok followers, Addison posted a video of herself and her boyfriend Omer hugging and kissing in the car as she showed off her snake tattoo placed on her foot.

Omer, who rocked a sunhat however, looked unrecognizable as fans captured a glimpse of his newly shaved head.

The music producer has previously rocked long locks, including his iconic bright red hairstyle, which he’s shown off at many public events, including the 2022 Grammys Red Carpet.

Fans ask ‘who the other guy’ is

After the huge transformation, many fans were questioning who the ‘other guy’ was in Addison’s video, as they failed to recognize her long-term boyfriend.

“Bruh what did I miss what happen to the other guy?” commented one fan.

Another confused fan questioned: “Where’s Omer??”

Luckily, eagle-eyed fans were on hand to clear up any confusion, as one confirmed: “It’s the same guy, he just cut his hair.”

“He got a very good haircut,” wrote another.

Addison Rae and Omer Fedi’s relationship timeline

The couple was first spotted together in June 2021 at a Machine Gun Kelly concert. A few weeks later, Addison posted a ‘mystery’ video of two shadows kissing, which fans suspected was her and Omer.

After dropping several hints in the months leading up, the couple finally went official in August, as Fedi posted a cute couples photo to his Instagram story.

Cut to 2023, and the couple are still going strong after two years together.
