I lost 80lbs by eating this VERY strange meal every single day

A weight loss influencer has gone viral after sharing the very bizarre meal that she claims is the secret to her slimmed down physique.

Tiffany Magee, who stands at 5ft7in, previously weighed 230 pounds - but has since undergone a radical transformation after sticking to a single go-to meal.

The social media star, from Tampa, Florida, has gained more than 625,000 followers on TikTok by sharing the dish which consists of a chicken sausage, various crunchy vegetables, mustard and cottage cheese.

And others have been left stunned after Tiffany revealed that the combination has so far helped her shed more than 80lbs.

In one of her recent clips, which has been viewed more than three million times, Tiffany can be seen sitting in her car with her pre-prepared lunch in hand.

She chows down as she talks viewers through each component: 'Lunch today is a chicken sausage with mustard, a raw Brussel sprout with mustard, pickled ochre with some mustard, radish with mustard. [My] favorite thing in the world, hearts of palm with mustard.'

And, continuing to pick up the raw vegetables with her hands, Tiffany then eats broccoli and asparagus - both of which are also smothered in the cottage cheese and mustard sauce.

But the peculiar pairings do not stop there as the influencer tucks into slices of dragon fruit and Japanese white sweet potato.

She washes it all down with a bottle of watermelon electrolytes.

Tiffany has repeatedly uploaded clips of herself eating a similar, if not identical, combination which she credits to her weight loss.

And other social media users have been left stunned as they heaped on the praise.

One wrote: 'I'm about to spend the last of my bank account on these veggies and some mustard.'

But the social media star has attempted to cement her position once and for all by releasing her own line of merchandise as well as a cookbook titled Live, Laugh, Mustard.

Revealing further detail about her transformation on her blog, Tiffany wrote: 'I've struggled with weight loss since I was eight years old. 

'My whole family was overweight, and the way we ate made it impossible for me to be a healthy weight.

'By the time I was 15, I felt miserable and my confidence was shot. 

'I wanted to be fit, healthy, and happy but I was none of these.

'My feet were always hurting, I was tired 24/7, I had an uncontrollable hunger, always sweaty, I lived off mountain dew and sodas, and every meal I ate was boxed food or fast food. 

'No matter what clothes I wore, I felt so uncomfortable and embarrassed in my body.

'After 20 years of dieting and crashing, I finally learned how to lose weight without either starving myself, developing an eating disorder, or crashing after I dieted.'
