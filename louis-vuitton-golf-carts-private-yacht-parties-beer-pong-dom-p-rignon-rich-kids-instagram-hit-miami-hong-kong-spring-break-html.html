The Rich Kids of Instagram hit Miami and Hong Kong for spring break

It's Spring Break season which means hordes of college co-eds are heading south to drink, dance and be merry. 

But a package deal to Punta Cana or Cancun just won't do it for the progeny of the one percent. 

Yes, the Rich Kids of Instagram are out-doing everyone yet again with their private jets to lavish locations like the West Indies, Miami and Hong Kong.

In some regards though - their March getaways aren't too different from the Michigan State kids playing beer pong on the beach in Florida. 

The only difference is that kids like Alex Bostanian play with Dom Perignon, not Bud lite. 

The Armenian-born Los Angeles, California resident who describes himself as a 'world traveler, party starter and international socialite' posted a snap of his champagne bong set up on a picturesque beach in Malibu recently, writing 'Life's a beach, and I'm just slayin it!  One bottle at a time #dompong #champagnepong'
