Meet Actor Chukwudi Iwuji! His Bio, Partner, Family, Net Worth

Chukwudi Iwuji’s character “Clemson Murn” on Peacemaker died on its 7th episode of season 1, but fans weren’t going to miss him for long as he was already filming his next fiction movie Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3. So, meanwhile, we wait for the actor to appear on screen again, let’s delve into this “Chukwudi Iwuji Bio” to explore his partner, family, and net worth.

Chukwudi Iwuji On Peacemaker

Chukwudi Iwuji knew it was going to be a “sayonara” for his character in ep 7 of Peacemaker which aired in Feb 2022. “I got the script right from the start and knew his trajectory,” that actor shared in a Zoom interview.

Also, that actor went on to mention that if there were “breadcrumbs, it’s in the writing,” and not in his performance. He shared that he was “very careful to play it, moment to moment to moment.”

With that being said, although Chukwudi saw his ending from a mile away, he still was disappointed that he had to leave the HBO Max series. The actor even delivered a rendition of the classical Chopin song, “Prelude in E Minor (Op. 28, No.4),” paying a tribute to his character over IG.

Someday Chukwudi hopes that the comic book show can find a way to bring him back.  “My spirit will be with the team. If there are more seasons, this is DC. People die and come back,” he remarked.

Chukwudi Iwuji On Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3

As of 2022, Chukwudi Iwuji wasn’t allowed to reveal who he portrayed on Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3. However, he did say that his character gets a chance to interact with everyone in the Guardians main cast.

Also, back over one of his interviews, Chukwudi touched on some of the Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3 rumors, including him portraying Silver Surfer — “I saw those (rumors) recently, I’ve seen that. I saw Silver Surfer, I saw Nova, I’ve seen (many). For me to wrap my head around the fact that I am the subject of speculation around the Internet is kind of fun. But I have to stay mum, I can’t tell you. I’d love to.”

So, since Chukwudi didn’t deny his possible role as Silver Surfer (who was previously played by Laurence Fishburne in Fantastic Four: Rise of the Silver Surfer,) could he be the new Silver Surfer?

Well, speculations apart, let’s talk some facts about how he landed the role. You might be surprised but he didn’t go through any auditions or asked anyone to be in the movie. It was James Gun himself who approached him and said, “I want you to be (name omitted) in Guardians of the Galaxy 3.”

“And I remember it was one of the few genuine times of my life where I was speechless,” he recalled. Having the possibility/capability to hire anyone in the industry and the filmmaker chose Chukwudi, the actor was just baffled.

How Much Is Chukwudi Iwuji Net Worth?

Chukwudi Iwuji garnered a net worth of over $1 million by 2022.

The actor first started his career while he was still doing his major in Economics at Yale University. At the time, he was a part of the university’s drama society, and whilst appearing in their production of “Murder in the Cathedral” he won an acting scholarship at a drama school in Wisconsin.

So, Chukwudi completed drama school, moved back to the UK, auditioned for the Royal Shakespeare Company, and offers started flooding his way.

At the time of writing this article, Chukwudi had over 35 movies and TV shows credited to his name. Mentioning some of them are — The Split (2022), Designated Survivor (2019), Blindspot (2016), Wizards vs. Aliens (2012), Proof (2005), and many more.

Fun Fact: Chukwudi’s fans someday want to see him as “James Baldwin” in the author’s biopic.

Chukwudi Iwuji Partner

Chukwudi Iwuji’s partner Angela Travino is also a singer and a theater artist who appeared as “Alison,” in Theatre Raleigh’s show Fun Home (2021). The two were together at least since 2015 and held their wedding on February 2, 2020, after being engaged for 2 years and a month.

Commemorating their first wedding anniversary, Chukwudi took it to his IG to share, “Can’t believe it’s been one full revolution around the sun. You are and continue to be my main source of love, laughter, and inspiration. Don’t screw it up now.”

Here’s Angela’s IG @atravino and FB @travino.angela.

Reportedly, Chukwudi’s wife went to Ruth Asawa San Francisco School of the Arts and studied Musical theatre at Syracuse University.

Trivia: Angela receives her birthday wishes on January 9.

Has Chukwudi Iwuji Shared Details About His Family?

Chukwudi Iwuji is the son of two former United Nations Organization employees who moved to Ethiopia, East Africa for work. But they sent Chukwudi to a boarding school in England.

Joining Chukwudi in England, were his brother named Odiri and a sister named Nnenna Iwuji.

His brother, Odiri Iwuji is a UNC Greensboro graduate who worked at C21 Media. As of 2022, he resided in  London, United Kingdom with his wife Pamela Priest, a fashion designer by profession.

Did you know: The Iwuji siblings together ran a film production company Chudor House Productions.

Chukwudi Iwuji Pronunciation
