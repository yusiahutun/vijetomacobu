Heidi Klum causes a stir in leather thigh-high boots and unexpected mini skirt

The America's Got Talent star was pictured alongside fellow judges Sofia Vergara and Howie Mandel

    Heidi Klum isn't afraid to make a serious statement, even when she's off-duty. The German supermodel turned heads when she rocked up at America's Got Talent filming on Wednesday, looking picture-perfect in the coolest pair of thigh-high boots.

    The 49-year-old beauty caused a stir in the statement footwear as she arrived ahead of filming alongside her co-hosts Sofia Vergara and Howie Mandel, while Simon Cowell was nowhere to be seen. Heidi's soft white leather boots were perfectly paired with an on-trend denim cut-off miniskirt, proving that she nailed Y2K fashion first.

    She dressed the look down with a soft cotton hoodie, wearing the cozy sweater tucked into her low-key denim.

    In keeping with her casual aesthetic, Heidi wore her long blonde hair down in a loosely tousled style, adding a baseball cap and oversized sunglasses.

    The presenter was seen taking selfies with fans and dancing for the cameras as she made her way into filming.
