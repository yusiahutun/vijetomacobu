Lenny Kravitz posts workout video but fans can't get over his leather pants

Lenny Kravitz is known for his signature style, and he didn’t let a pair of leather pants get in the way of his workout.

The 59-year-old shared a video of his gym session on Instagram, and fans loved his commitment to staying fit. The music star couldn’t resist rocking up for the workout in classic Lenny Kravitz style – so he ensured his leather pants came with him! It’s a fashion statement, for sure.

Lenny Kravitz didn’t work up a sweat in leather pants

The celebrity shared the video of him lifting weights whilst also performing crunches – and appeared as though it was like a walk in the park.

The musician captioned the video: “Thank God for today! Grateful. Never been better.

“There are no shortcuts, so seize your day. It is all possible. Love!”

Alongside the classic Lenny Kravitz leather pants statement, he wore a tank top, black sunglasses and gloves for the weights. He also had high black boots on.

Fans loved his commitment, with one writing: “Lenny Kravitz worked out in a fishnet top, leather pants and boots so now so do I”.

Edwin McCain joked: “Watching while eating Oreos”.

Lenny finally receives a Hollywood star

It comes after the Fly Away hitmaker received his star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame for his career in entertainment.

The Hunger Games actor has managed to cross over between both worlds of movie and music over the decades.

He said at the time: “It’s an incredible honor to be on this street. To be with all of these great people that have done incredible things with their lives is an honor.

“I never thought about it. Like I said, when I was a kid, I was walking up and down these streets but I was pretty much always thinking about where I was going to sleep that night.

“To have it now just feels so surreal and I’m really grateful.”
