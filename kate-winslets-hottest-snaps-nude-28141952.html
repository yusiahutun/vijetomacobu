Kate Winslet’s striking red dress was captured while she was filming for Romance and Cigarettes.

The bright crimson colour contrasted her auburn hair and showed a lot of skin as she ran through the streets.

Her dress was perfectly complimented by a matching pair of peep toe heels and a full face of makeup with a red lipstick.

The latex dress featured halter neck straps and cups around her chest while the fabric sucked her in around the waist, exposing her hourglass shape.

The long skirt flared out and reached just above her ankles, but featured a thigh high split that showed off her toned legs.

Babe in Blue

This blue number clung to Kate’s stunning figure as she posed with her hands on her hips as she walked the red carpet for the UK premiere of A Little Chaos back in 2015.

She wore her blonde hair in a classy up do which showed off her facial features, perfectly accentuated with minimal makeup and a light pink lipstick.

The dress had a plunging neckline and onlookers were treated with a glimpse of the actress’ assets as she gazed off into the distance.

She wore strappy black and gold heels for the premiere and drew attention from all angles as the cobalt blue contrasted with her skin tone.

In The Nude

Arguably the most defining role of her career, Kate captured audiences across the world when she played Rose in 1997’s Titanic.

She had an easily identifiable look of curly ginger hair and doll-like features, but perhaps her most memorable look came in the “paint me like your French girls” scene.

Jack Dawson, played by Leonardo DiCaprio, painted Rose as she laid on a lavish sofa wearing nothing but the Heart of the Ocean pendant.

Kate posed with her arms above her head and stared deep into Leo’s eyes as he sketched her figure and audiences were left with an image they’d never forget.

Stunning in Silver

Kate turned it up a notch at the 82nd Academy Awards back in 2010 when she wore a sparkly silver strapless number.

The dress had a tight, corset style fit and the lack of straps brought attention to her chest and toned arms.

She styled the look with glamorous yet minimal makeup and old Hollywood curls, channelling her inner Marilyn Monroe with her blonde, face framing locks.

Winslet simply accessorised with a chunky silver chain necklace with a large, eye-catching silver diamond pendant in the middle.

Simple Glam

Back in 2019, Kate swept audiences away as she attended the 73rd Emmy Awards in a figure hugging black dress.

The piece featured her signature plunging neckline and sheer floaty sleeves that stopped just above her elbows.

She flashed her usual sassy pose as she held one hip with her hand while the other clutched a luxury black bag.

Kate’s blonde hair was parted in the middle and fell down the sides of her face as she smiled sweetly for the cameras, her gorgeous good looks brought out with a touch of blusher and mascara.

Model Material

Kate was part of a campaign for American clothing brand St. John and the actress could easily switch careers as she looked breathtaking throughout the photoshoot.

She wore a body con dress with long sleeves that reached down to her calf and helped show off her curvaceous figure.

Winslet clasped her manicured hands together as she made eyes with the camera and gave a subtle pout as she laid on her hip.

Kate’s voluminous blonde hair towered above her head and tumbled over her shoulders in tight ringlet curls.
