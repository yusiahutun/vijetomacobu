Zendaya's Low-Cut Goddess Gown Nearly Plunged to Her Belly Button

If there's one thing we can say about Zendaya's style, it's that it doesn't fit into any single box. One day, she'll be wearing a classic LBD, and the next...a full-on robot suit. In other words, the girl has range, and just yesterday, she showed off her sartorial versatility once again while sporting two very different looks in one night. 

First, Zendaya attended the Essence Black Women in Hollywood award ceremony dressed in a sheer silver corset with delicate floral embroidery and paired with baggy medium-wash cuffed jeans. She accessorized silver hoops, cuffs, armbands, and two belts — however, the pièce de résistance of her outfit was a silver choker from Bulgari that covered the entirety of her neck. Meanwhile, glowing skin, glossy lips, and a flippy bob provided the finishing touches to her red carpet glam.

Barely giving us a moment to catch our breath, Zendaya stepped out for her second event of the evening in another show-stopping look. Arriving at W Magazine and Louis Vuitton's Academy Awards Dinner at Mr. Chow in Los Angeles, the actress looked like a total bombshell in a pleated periwinkle gown with an extreme plunging neckline that scooped down to her waist. She teamed the backless maxi-length dress with black pointed-toe pumps, silver jewelry, and a leather wrap belt, while soft curls styled with a sweeping side part rounded out her look.

Just your classic case of too much fashion, too little time.
