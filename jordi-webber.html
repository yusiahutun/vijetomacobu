Biography Timeline

Jordi Webber is a New Zealand actor, musician, and dancer known for his film, television, and theatre work. Here is a timeline of some of the significant events in his life and career:

  • 1990: Jordi Webber was born on February 25, in Auckland, New Zealand.
  • 2008: He auditioned for the New Zealand version of the reality show “So You Think You Can Dance,” where he made it to the Top 18.
  • 2012: Jordi made his acting debut in the New Zealand TV series “The Almighty Johnsons,” where he played the role of “Olaf Johnson” for three seasons.
  • 2014: He played the lead role of “Ben Gallagher” in the famous New Zealand musical “The Factory,” which earned him critical acclaim.
  • 2015: Jordi starred in the New Zealand film “Born to Dance,” where he played the role of “Gabriel” and contributed to the film’s soundtrack.
  • 2016: He played the role of “Finn” in the New Zealand TV series “Westside.”
  • 2017: Jordi released his debut single, “Fire,” followed by his debut EP “Symmetry”, in 2019.
  • 2019: He played the role of “John” in the New Zealand production of the musical “Miss Saigon.”
  • 2021: Jordi joined the cast of the Australian soap opera “Neighbours,” where he plays the role of “Hendrix Greyson.”
