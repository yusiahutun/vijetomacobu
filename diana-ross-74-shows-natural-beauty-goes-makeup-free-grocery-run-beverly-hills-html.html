Diana Ross, 74, shows her natural beauty as she goes makeup-free on grocery run in Beverly Hills

She's a music legend.

But Diana Ross, at 74, isn't above doing her own grocery shopping and pushing her own loaded cart back to her car.

The Motown superstar, who was not wearing makeup and sported her trademark mass of curls, looked fabulous as she stopped by Bristol Farms in Beverly Hills on Sunday.  

Motown legend: Diana Ross, 74, showed her natural beauty as she ran errands makeup-free and sporting her trademark mass of curls in Beverly Hills on Sunday

Ross smiled happily as she made her way through the parking lot with her purchases in brown paper store bags.

She was dressed in a black sweater and loose black trousers and stepped out in brown Ugg boots.

The singer shows no signs of slowing down.

Earlier this month, she performed in Ottawa, Canada, helping the National Arts Centre Foundation raise more than $780,000 for young musicians.

And on Wednesday, she kicks off a series of nine concerts at Wynn Las Vegas.

Ross's five children have all followed their famous mother into the entertainment industry.

Her most well-known kids are Tracee Ellis-Ross and Evan Ross.

Tracee, 46, currently stars on the sitcom Black-ish while actor and musician Evan, 30, who is married to Ashlee Simpson, is starring with his wife in a new reality series and the couple also recently released an EP together.

Daughter Chudney Ross, 42, works as a film producer as does her son Ross Naess, 31, while daughter Rhonda Ross Kendrick, 47, is a singer and songwriter. 

Through her children, Ross is also a grandmother of five.
