    After Chanel presented its cruise 2023 collection on a beach in Monte Carlo last week, Louis Vuitton headed to San Diego for its destination presentation.

    The fashion house showed its cruise collection at the Salk Institute in California, where creative director Nicolas Ghesquière continued his tradition of staging Louis Vuitton cruise shows at some of the world’s most remarkable architectural structures. The Louis Kahn-designed Brutalist masterpiece was chosen as the latest venue, and drew in a very starry front row, which included Lea Seydoux, Miranda Kerr, Gemma Chan and Emma Roberts, among others.

    Below, we round up the catwalk and front-row highlights you need to see from Louis Vuitton cruise 2023.
