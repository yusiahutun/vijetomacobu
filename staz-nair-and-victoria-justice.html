About

Victoria Justice is a 31 year old American Actress. Born Victoria Dawn Justice on 19th February, 1993 in Hollywood, Florida, USA and educated at Hollywood Arts High School and Sherwood High School, she is famous for her roles as Rebecca in the Disney Channel sitcom The Suite Life of Zack and Cody, Lola Martinez in the Nickelodeon comedy-drama series Zoey 101, Shelby Marx in the Nickelodeon teen sitcom iCarly, Tori Vega in the Nickelodeon teen musical sitcom Victorious, Jordan Sands in the 2010 Nickelodeon comedy horror television film The Boy Who Cried Werewolf, Jane Harmon in the 2012 teen romantic comedy film The First Time, Wren DeSantis in the 2012 teen comedy film Fun Size, Naomi Mills in the 2015 romantic comedy-drama film Naomi and Ely's No Kiss List, Lindy Sampson in the MTV cyber thriller series Eye Candy, Janet Weiss in the 2016 Fox musical horror-comedy television film The Rocky Horror Picture Show: Let's Do the Time Warp Again, Jodie Schellenberger in the 2017 teen comedy film The Outcasts, Courtney Ingles in the YouTube/Facebook Watch satirical comedy series The Real Bros of Simi Valley, Brooke Gatwick in the 2021 erotic romance drama film Trust, Cassie Garcia in the 2021 Netflix supernatural comedy film Afterlife of the Party, Lola Alvarez in the 2022 Netflix romantic comedy film A Perfect Pairing, and Annie in the 2023 psychological thriller film The Tutor, and for her successful musical career and various soundtrack albums such as Spectacular!, Victorious, Victorious 2.0, and Victorious 3.0 in a career that spans 2009–present and Musical career and 2003–present.
