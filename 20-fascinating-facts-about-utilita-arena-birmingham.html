FAQs

1. What is the capacity of Arena Birmingham?

Arena Birmingham has a seating capacity of approximately 15,800 for concerts and can accommodate up to 12,700 for sporting events.

2. How can I get to Arena Birmingham?

Arena Birmingham is conveniently located in the heart of the city, making it easily accessible by public transport, car, or on foot. The nearest train station is Birmingham New Street, which is just a short walk away.

3. Can I bring my own food and drinks to Arena Birmingham?

Outside food and drinks are not allowed into the venue. However, there are a wide variety of food and beverage options available within the arena, offering something for everyone’s taste.

4. Is Arena Birmingham accessible for people with disabilities?

Arena Birmingham is fully accessible for people with disabilities, with designated seating areas, accessible facilities, and assistance for those with mobility issues. There are also dedicated parking spaces available for disabled visitors.

5. Are there hotels near Arena Birmingham?

Yes, there are several hotels within close proximity to Arena Birmingham, offering convenient accommodation options for visitors attending events at the venue.
