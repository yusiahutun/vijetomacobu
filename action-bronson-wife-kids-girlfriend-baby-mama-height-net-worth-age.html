Action Bronson Career

After graduating from the Art Institute, Bronson, who was very close to his parents’ restaurant, began his career as a chef. He later worked as a master chef at a fire-flame gourmet, restaurant in New York City. He also started an online cooking show called Action in the Kitchen and later published a cookbook called Fuck, That’s Delicious. During one of his shows, Bronson encountered an accident in the kitchen that resulted in a leg injury.

After this incident, Action Bronson decided to focus on his career as a rapper. In March 2011 he released his first studio album entitled Dr. Lecter under Fine Fabric Delegates. At that time he did not have a record deal; his songs were produced by a New York-based producer named Tommy Mas. In 2012 he signed with Warner Bros. Records and later released other albums and mixtapes, including Well-Done in 2011, Blue Chips 2 in 2013, and his debut album Mr. Wonderful in 2015. Bronson also released his big hit album called Saab Stories.

In addition, the talented rapper launched his own self-moderated TV shows such as Fuck, That’s Delicious, The Untitled Action Bronson Show, and Traveling the Stars: Action Bronson and Friends Watch ‘Ancient Aliens’.

Following his unique rap style and wonderful performances, Bronson has been nominated twice for the BET Hip Hop Awards, firstly as Rockie of the Year in 2013, and his album Blue Chips 2 was nominated in the Best Mixtape category in 2014. He is currently under contract with Vice Records and Atlantic Records.
