What happened to Jill Su?

Where did Jill Halliburton live? Jill Halliburton, aged 59, was found dead in the bathtub of her luxurious Florida residence on September 28, 2014, by her son, Justin Su. At the time, Justin, a student at Broward College, informed the authorities that his mother had taken her own life. He said to the Broward County's 911 dispatcher,

"She just stabbed herself and threw herself in the bathtub."

But after looking closely at his mother and noticing her bound hands, he retracted his statement. He told the 911 dispatcher,

"She looks so cut up. Someone killed her."

Justin had phoned his father, Professor Nan Yao Su, who immediately rushed to his Florida home from his office. The couple had recently returned from a trip to Asia, after which Nan resumed his work routine while Jill remained home to recharge.

Why was Jill Su's son, Justin, considered a person of interest?

The police officers found an alarm box and a serrated blade in the bathtub and sent them to the forensic lab for analysis. Additionally, another knife was discovered just outside the residence's front entrance. They also collected Justin's blood-stained clothes as potential evidence and drove him and his father to the station for interrogation.

According to the New York Post, investigators uncovered that Justin Su had a penchant for collecting knives, which made him a key suspect in the murder case. This suspicion was further fueled by Justin's inconsistent narratives regarding the events leading up to his mother's demise.

During a separate police interview, Nan Yao disclosed that something was amiss even before receiving his son's call while he was at work. He confessed to seeing a figure inside his residence from a remote security app before the feed was abruptly shut down.
