Taylor Swift wears adorable but tiny earrings in tribute to beau Travis Kelce

The singer is the queen of Easter eggs. Not just for her era-winning music, but tiny clues she likes to hide in her lyrics for fans. But Taylor Swift recently wore tiny earrings that are a small tribute to herself and Travis Kelce. They were so small, fans may have missed them.

The ‘It couple’ of the year, Travis Kelce and Taylor Swift have taken over the Internet. Their dreamy love story started with Travis putting Taylor ‘on a blast’ in a podcast episode during the summer. Sometimes, it does not take long to find The 1 and Travis Kelce’s ‘green flags‘ were enough for the music star to know that she has a keeper. Hence in typical Tay Tay style, the singer is showing her support for the NFL heartthrob and the Internet can’t have enough of it.

Taylor Swift hides a tiny tribute to Travis in plain sight

The Grammy-winning celebrity knows her eagle-eyed fans love a good challenge, and she was seen recently hiding tiny tributes for her beau in plain sight. The Champagne Problems crooner recently attended the Kansas City Chiefs and the Las Vegas Raiders match on December 25.

Tay Tay’s ‘supportive girlfriend’ accessories included a festive Santa hat with the number “87” on it and a cute T-initial earring on her left earlobe. The tiny earring is a nod to her matching initial with Travis.

Tay’s match day outfit also included a signature red cable knit sweater and a plaid mini skirt over patterned tights.

‘Call It What You Want’

Taylor Swift’s new accessory for Travis is reminding fans of her popular song, Call It What You Want from Reputation.

A fan commented on TikTok: “I want to wear his initials on my earlobes.”

Another fan wrote: “She’s wearing two earrings, so T and T is Taylor and Travis.”

A third fan theory suggests the music legend could be also hinting at the upcoming Reputation’s Taylor Version release through this particular Easter egg.
