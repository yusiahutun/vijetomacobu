Love Island viewers praise Laura Whitmore for getting 'dress right' during reunion

Love Island fans have praised Laura Whitmore's reunion outfit as the "best she's worn throughout the entire series".

Wearing a stunning bubblegum pink Barbie-esque dress, Laura put on an ultra leggy display as she dressed to impress for the reunion show that aired on Sunday, August 7.

Fans were quick to gush over Laura's glam display, with many taking to Twitter to say that her outfit was the best she's worn all season long.

Another viewer then harshly critiqued: "why is laura the best dressed she's been all series, and its a boring pink dress?"

While a third echoed: "This is Laura’s first decent dress."

And a fourth said: "Laura stepped up her fashion she looks amaze #LoveIsland."

This comes after Laura donned a divisive Versace jumpsuit for the final last Monday (August 1).

Fans weren't too impressed with Laura's ensemble last week, but this week was a different story as most viewers seemed to approve.

During the reunion, which aired on Sunday night (August 7), Laura was called out by fans for not asking Paige Thorne about her ex love interest Jacques O'Neill.

Talking to stunning Welsh paramedic Paige and her bombshell beau Adam Collard, Laura showed an awkward VT of Paige's "unusual" noises - but it wasn't what viewers at home wanted to see.

Many called out the Irish sweetheart for asking the "wrong questions" and missing out on what viewers wanted.

"#loveisland laura didn’t even ask Paige about Jacques???! she’s such a useless host get her off," fumed one viewer.

Another added: "Laura at least address the Jacques/Paige/Adam beef PLEASEEEE FFS."

"Genuinely don’t care laura ask Paige about Jacques #loveisland," echoed a third.
