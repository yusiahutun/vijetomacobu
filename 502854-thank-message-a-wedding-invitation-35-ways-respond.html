Thank you message for a wedding invitation: 35 ways to respond

Weddings are special occasions, and people only want to celebrate them with their loved ones. When you are invited, understand that you mean so much in their lives. Therefore, when you receive such an invitation, show appreciation using the best thank you message for a wedding invitation.

Are you looking for the best thank you message for a wedding invitation? To some people, crafting a good message is a challenging task. Nonetheless, there are numerous examples you can draw inspiration from.

Thank you message for a wedding invitation

How do you say thank you after wedding invitation? Replying to an invitation is a show of appreciation. Fortunately, there are numerous ways of doing it, including sending thank you messages. Below are some thank you message for inviting you to a wedding that you can use.

  • I’m so happy that you found your soul mate. Thanks for the Wedding Invitation. We will definitely be a part of your special day.
  • I'm looking forward to witnessing your and your love exchange of vows. Thanks for the wedding invitation.
  • Thank you for the invitation to your wedding ceremony. I am looking forward to joining you in sharing your happiness.
  • Thank you for inviting me to your wedding. The invitation was beautiful, and I’m looking forward to seeing all our college friends there.
  • We are so excited to attend your wedding on [the date]. Thank you for inviting us! We are looking forward to celebrating your big day!
  • I am so excited about the wedding invitation. So glad to know that I will be joining you at your wedding.
  • It would be great to see such a fantastic couple getting married. Thanks for the wedding invitation.
  • Thank you for inviting me to your wedding. The invitation card was beautiful, and I’m looking forward to seeing all our friends there.
  • I hope your wedding makes it to the definition of being perfect! I can't thank you enough for the wedding invitation.
  • I received your wedding invitation and will be attending. Thanks so much for your consideration.
  • I am super excited to attend your wedding and celebrate the day. Thank you for inviting our whole family! We’re looking forward to it!
  • I appreciate the invitation to your wedding. Thanks for considering me as part of your great function. See you soon m,y dear.
  • Thanks for the invite to your wedding reception on June 4th. My spouse and I will both be attending and are looking forward to it.

A thank you note for an invitation to a wedding

There are many ways to respond to an invitation. You can reply to a wedding invitation by email or by text message. Below are some thank you notes for an invitation to a wedding.

  • Congratulations to you and your family on the upcoming ceremony. Thanks for the beautiful wedding invitation.
  • I am really happy that you are planning to settle down. I will attend your wedding to witness this important milestone.
  • We are excited to attend your wedding on July 5th. All four of us will be coming. Thank you for inviting us! We are looking forward to it. See you then.
  • It’s an honour to witness the official unification of your lives. Thank you for having me. Much love and blessings!
  • From the bottom of my heart, I thank you for your invitation and wish you all the best now and in the future.
  • I received your beautiful invitation today. And I am delighted that you have considered me important to be a part of your great moment. I appreciate the invitation so much!
  • I can't wait to attend your wedding and watch you become a married couple. I thank you for the wedding invitation, my dear.
  • Thank you for your wedding invitation. I look forward to a beautiful and fulfilling ceremony. I wish you the best.
  • Please accept my thanks and appreciation for the invitation. I have never been so excited to receive such an invitation. I look forward to attending the event.
  • I am so thankful for your kind invitation and for letting us be a part of your most precious day ever. Wishing you a long and prosperous life with the love of your life.
  • Thank you for inviting us to be a part of your special day. May your wedding day be the start of a lifetime of happiness together.

How to thank but decline a wedding invitation

If someone invites you to their wedding, but your schedule does not allow you time to attend, you need to communicate with them as well. Here are some of the polite ways of declining their invitation.

  • Thank you both so much for including us in your wedding. Sadly, we won't be able to attend, as we will be travelling to the UK for my nephew's christening. I wish we could be in two places at once!
  • Thank you for the wedding invitation, but unfortunately, I'm sorry I won't be there in person, but I'm sending my best wishes to you both.
  • Thank you so much for inviting us to your wedding next month. We appreciate the invitation. However, we will not be able to come. We wish you the best on your big day.
  • Thanks so much for the invitation. Sadly, due to (family/work/financial commitments), I won't be able to make it. However, I wish you the best on that day.
  • Thank you for thinking of me. I regret to tell you that I won't be able to attend due to another commitment, but please accept my warmest congratulations.
  • While I’d love to be with you on your special day, I’m sadly unable to attend. I will be there in spirit and can not wait to see the photos! Thanks for considering me on your list of invitees.
  • Regretfully we are unable to attend. Best wishes on your special day!
  • Sadly, I am unable to attend your wedding day. However, I look forward to celebrating the next time we see you!
  • Thank you for inviting us to your wedding on March 21st. Unfortunately, we will not be able to attend as we will be away on vacation that week. We wish you all the best as you start your new life together.
  • Thank you so much for the invitation; I really appreciate it, and it means a great deal. I would love to attend, but I have prior commitments on that date.
