I Cant Stop Wearing This Stackable Gold Signet Ring From GLDN

When I find a piece of jewelry I truly love, you won’t catch me without it. That goes for my miraculously durable Amazon nameplate necklace, the Sporty & Rich tennis racquet chain that hasn’t left my chest since it arrived in the mail, and now, a personalized GLDN Slim Signet Ring that’s delicate enough to stack even with chunkier styles. 

Now because I rarely take off these staple pieces, I always reach for quality jewelry that won’t tarnish or scratch after consistent wear. When GLDN sent me a 14k gold-filled Signet Ring to try out, I was sold for that very reason. 

GLDN Slim Signet Ring 

The dainty ring features a non-cheesy, tiny “M” initial stamped into the signet and is constructed ofa high-quality brass core that’s coated in a thick layer of 14-karat gold. And while the gold-filled design isn’t quite as indestructible as, say, the solid gold option, it contains much more real gold than a regular plated piece and can withstand daily wear. In just the few weeks it’s spent at home on my finger, the Slim Signet Ring has proven to be the right choice for long-lasting jewelry that won’t break the bank. I’ve worn it consistently to play tennis, workout, wash the dishes, and shower without so much as a scratch. 

The option to customize the Signet Ring, and other GLDN styles for that matter, adds a personal touch to the piece—whether you’re sporting the bling yourself or gifting it to someone else. It’s an easy way to upgrade an otherwise simple look while keeping it understated for everyday wear. Jennifer Lopez made her own case for personalized jewelry earlier this year, while Hailey Bieber was similarly spotted in a pair of “J” initial earrings in honor of her husband, Justin. 

You don’t have to take my word for it either. Fans are similarly obsessed with the tiny signet ring style. One shopper, who owns both the Slim Signet and Micro Signet, said the rings “stand the test of time,” and they “never” take the styles off. Another customer raved about the “wallet-friendly” price tag, which makes it manageable to buy multiples for stacking without “breaking the bank,” while others praised the “dainty” and “timeless” piece that “hasn’t tarnished” despite constant wear. 

Whether you’re planning to wear the Slim Signet all on its own, or have every intention of stacking with other styles (like the Micro Signet Ring, perhaps), head over to GLDN and grab the 14-karat gold-filled ring for $65 with code GLDN15.
