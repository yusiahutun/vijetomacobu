Wild moment parents brawl at youth wrestling meet while children watch the chaos

This is the wild moment parents brawled at a youth wrestling match while their children watched the chaos from feet away. 

Videos showed the parents shoving, pulling and kicking each other at Phillipsburg High School's gym in New Jersey on Sunday afternoon as a youth wrestling match was underway in the foreground. 

The altercation began when two men - one in a white shirt and hat and the other wearing a black hoodie - started shouting at each other. Some people rushed over to intervene and separate the warring parties but the situation escalated.

Footage from a different angle showcases a disturbing view of at least a dozen parents engaging in the heated exchange. 

At least one child was injured during the brawl, a parent told lehighvalleylive.com under the condition their name be withheld.

The altercation erupted during the New Jersey grade school wrestling championships, as Phillipsburg's youth team faced off against High Point School's team. 

While young wrestlers Anderson Diaz and Alexander Finklestein grappled on the mat, a disturbance unfolded among adults in the background.

The video captured a man in a white shirt and hat shoving another in a black hoodie and hat, escalating into a face-to-face confrontation. 

The shouting attracted the attention of other spectators, including children and parents, who rushed down from the stands in an attempt to intervene.

Amidst the chaos, a man wearing a yellow shirt and jeans was forcefully shoved to the ground and kicked in the head. 

The initial instigators fell onto the mat, as others desperately tried to separate them.

The unfortunate incident resulted in the match between the two young wrestlers being halted, as referees rushed to the scene to restore order.
