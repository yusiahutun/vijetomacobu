Nadia Nakai's biography

The rapper was born on 18th May 1990 in South Africa. She was born to a South African father and a Zimbabwean mother. However, she has not offered any information about her parents' names and current whereabouts. Likewise, there is no publicly known information about her siblings.

Nadia attended Fourways High School in Johannesburg, South Africa, for a year before moving to Kenya, where she completed her studies. After her high school education, she enrolled at Monash University, Johannesburg, for a Degree in Marketing, Communication and Media Studies.

Music career

Nakai began her career by appearing on the e.tv hip hop show, Shiz Niz. She won the Mixtape 101 competition, making her the first female to win the competition. However, she rose to fame in September 2013 after releasing her debut single, Like Me.

Nadia Nakai's boyfriend and dating history

The South African rapper is in a relationship with Kiernan Jarryd Forbes, commonly known by his stage name, AKA. He is a South African rapper, singer, songwriter, record producer, and entrepreneur. He was born and raised in Cape Town, Western Cape.

The duo made their relationship public on 4th March 2022 via their personal Instagram accounts. This is AKA's first public relationship since her fiancee Anele Nellie Tember passed away in April 2021. Previously, Nadia was in a relationship with:

The rapper was once signed to Cassper's record label, Family Tree. The couple developed a close relationship leaving many of their fans confused. Nyovest and Nadia spent lots of quality time together in the studio. They also collaborated on hits such as Naah Mean and Ragga Ragga.
