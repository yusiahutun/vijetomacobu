Caylan Clossick sought by Manchester police mocked over distinctive ears

A teenager wanted in connection to a theft is being roasted on the internet after his big eared mugshot was shared online.

Greater Manchester Police's Wigan East division are looking to track down Caylan Clossick amid allegations he was involved in an act of stealing in the area on November 3.

To help with their investigation, they posted a previously taken custody snap of the 18-year-old on their Facebook page on Monday.

Little could they have imagined, however, how far their local appeal would travel.

The teenager's ear-heavy photo has been shared more than 17,000 since web pranksters decided to jump on the wanted man's somewhat protruding lobes.

Clossick's large lugs have since become the subject of more than 27,000 comments and spawned a whole catalogue of big ear memes and mock-ups.
