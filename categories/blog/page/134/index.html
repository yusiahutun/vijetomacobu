Drunk Irkutsk man loses arm to bear in Russia

published on July 28, 2024
A caged brown bear has bitten off the arm of a 'drunk' man who tried to feed the animal at a Russian roadside cafe.  Andrey Sakharovsky, 42, was rushed to hospital after the terrifying incident, which occurred in Siberia's Irkutsk region. Staff had sold Mr Sakharovsky cans of condensed milk in order to feed the three brown bears kept by the cafe to attract customers. CCTV footage shows Mr Sakharovsky clambering over a fence to reach the cage followed by screams of terror - he was passing the cans through the bars when one of the bears grabbed his arm.
