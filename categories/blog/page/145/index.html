Pick a Dried Floral Wreath To Spruce Up Your Front Door

published on July 23, 2024
Nothing is more welcoming to guests than a colorful, floral wreath hanging on your front door. It’s also an easy way to decorate without being an interior designer — just hang it on an over-the-door hook and voilà! You look like someone who has their life together, someone who decorates. Dried floral wreaths are especially lovely. Often handmade with natural materials, they can last months with the right care.
