CAA Promotes 21 Trainees To Agent/Executive

published on July 11, 2024
LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) – Creative Artists Agency announced today at its annual company retreat that it has promoted 21 trainees to agent/executive. Barry Buren, Carly Fromm, Nikki Goldfarb, Ilana Goren, Jacob Schiff, and Jiah Shin have been promoted to agents in CAA’s Television division. Based in CAA’s Los Angeles office, Goren, Schiff, and Shin will serve in Scripted Television, with Buren in Television Talent.
