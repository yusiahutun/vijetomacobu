Hannah Murray Boyfriend, Dating, Interview

EntertainmentBy LRU Writer | On: April 24, 2019

Not every celeb wants to publicize their relationship and other details of their personal life. But that doesn’t mean that there will be no alleged link-ups and break-ups. No matter what, the person's private and professional life both will make the headlines. And same goes to Gilly from Game of Thrones, Hannah Murray.

The first thing one has in their head when they think about Hannah Murray is most probably her dating affairs. People often want to know about the relationship status of the English actress on whether she is dating a boyfriend or just busy with her work for GOT. Well, have no worries as this sheds light on the personal life of Hannah Murray!

Boyfriend Talks: Dating Anyone?

Hannah has won the hearts of many with her sizzling performance portraying the character of Cassie Ainsworth in Skins and Gilly in Game Of Thrones. After her work in several renowned shows, with no doubts, she is in the limelight and gains all the attention.

In that case, it will be hard for public figures to hide their private life from the eye of people. And it seems that Hannah has been handling in a pretty good way.

After a decade in the entertainment industry, this Bristol native hasn’t yet made the headlines relating to her dating affairs. She has handled her personal life in a better way and is doing so till date. But, does that mean she has never been in a dating relationship and is still single?

Sources report Hannah to be single, but who knows, she might be enjoying her time with a secret boyfriend.

The girl this beautiful must have a hard time remaining single, right?

Is Hannah Pregnant?

When HBO commenced airing the final season Game of Thrones in April 2019, fans couldn't help but discern Hannah's weight gain. It led to the assumption that she could be pregnant.

Without dropping any names, British tabloids reported that the Gold Help The Girl star was out on a romantic date with an alleged boyfriend. Per the reports, the actress didn't hesitate to showcase her baby bump.

Neither Hannah nor her representative has opted to chime in on the rumor.

Game Of Thrones Journey; Raves About Co-Star In Interview

Hannah Murray got cast as Gilly in the most watched HBO’s show Game of Thrones in August 2011 for the second and third season.

Followed by the work in GOT, she landed in several roles in several films as well. In 2017, she starred in the crime drama film, Detroit.

As the most awaited penultimate season of GOT aired on HBO in April 2019, it is evident that Hannah will be there till the end.

She is very connected and has a special bonding with her GOT cast members. In an interview, she has mentioned about her on-screen match-up John Bradley, where she said,

“John likes to read me funny tweets in the car on the way home.”

She explained,

“Like pretentious actor-y things.”

Hannah further added,

"I think it's good to laugh at them, because then you can deflate their egos a bit."

John portrays the character of Sam in the beloved HBO series. Together, Gilly and Sam make an adorable couple.

Hannah Murphy with on-screen beau, John Bradley on Game Of Thrones (Photo:- elitedaily.com)

Loved by many, the duo shared a similar childhood experience -- with both of them suffering abuse. A director of the series linked the characters as "two birds with two broken wings."
