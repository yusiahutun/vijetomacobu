Vevo Announces DSCVR Artists To Watch 2020

NEW YORK (CelebrityAccess) – Vevo has today (Nov. 4) released the complete list for its highly-anticipated Vevo DSCVR ‘Artists to Watch’ campaign, which champions emerging artists, through performance content.

Now in its sixth year, the list consists of 20 artists who Vevo believes will breakthrough in the coming year.

Past alumni of Vevo’s DSCVR ‘Artists To Watch’ series include Billie Ellish, Alessia Cara, Little Simz, SlowThai and Lewis Capaldi among others.

JP Evangelista, SVP of Content, Programming & Marketing, said: “DSCVR Artists to Watch is a key program for Vevo and now in its 6th year demonstrates our commitment to championing emerging artists. The Vevo team takes great pride in fostering new talent and helping expose our audience to artists we feel will make an impact in the forthcoming year. We’re excited to shine a light on these 20 artists, and watch as they continue to grow their fan base into 2020.”

The 2020 Vevo ‘Artists to Watch’ are: Aitch, Arlo Parks, Awa, Babyjake, Beba, Cautious Clay, Celeste, Conan, Gray, Guaynaa, Joy Crookes, Layton Greene, Liily, Lunay, Matt Stell, No Rome, NSG, Pop Smoke, Rilès, Sports Team, Vitão.

The program launches today (Nov. 4) with two exclusive performances from pop artist Conan Gray, and will see Vevo premiering two performances from one artist per day through November 29th.
