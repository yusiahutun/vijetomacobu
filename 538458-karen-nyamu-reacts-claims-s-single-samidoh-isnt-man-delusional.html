Karen Nyamu Reacts to Claims She's Single, Samidoh Isn't Her Man: &quot;Delusional Much&quot;

  • Rumours of Karen Nyamu and Samidoh's breakup emerged after the singer travelled to the US to visit his first family in early January
  • Nyamu shared a post dismissing separation rumours, claiming people were delusional
  • Samidoh, while in the US, didn't share photos on social media with his wife and children, leaving a section of his fans disappointed

Karen Nyamu has shared a post seemingly responding to rumours that she is no longer with Samidoh after he recently returned to the country from the US.

Has Karen Nyamu been dumped by Samidoh?

Gossip mills claimed that Nyamu and Samidoh had parted ways, with some insinuating that the nominated senator's latest cryptic messages were directed to the Mugithi singer.

"I am single, but there is a man who is not mine, but because of this man, I don't give chances to others."

Karen Nyamu responds to heartbreak claims

A few weeks ago, fans of the nominated senator claimed she was heartbroken and only acting like she was okay after Samidoh reunited with his first family in the US.

"How's heartbreak taking you in this life?" a fan asked.

Responding to the fan, she said she was happy for Edday and her kids and wasn't upset, as many claim.

"Hehehe kutembelea watoto ni kitu inawapeanga heartbreak ama nafaa nifurahi sasa sitakua najitetea mkiniona na yeye kila saa kuniambia nilifanya atupe watoto (Is Visiting children something that can break my heart? Or should I be happy now that I won't be defending myself when you see me with him all the time telling me I made him desert his children)" she said.

Karen Nyamu and Samidoh a perfect match?

On January 22, a day after the father of five landed in the US, Nyamu shared a photo after returning to Nairobi from China, and netizens flooded the comment section, bringing up her baby daddy, Samidoh and his wife, Edday.

"When you were away, Samidoh flew to Boston to visit team nothing but prayers," a fan told Nyamu.

Responding to them, the mother of three said:

"Both he and the babies needed that. We are happy for them."

Another fan wrote:

"Samidoh says hello from Boston, USA."

The vocal politician revealed that they were in communication.

"We are very much in communication, mama."
