The History of Cd Alcoyano

Cd Alcoyano, short for Club Deportivo Alcoyano, is a Spanish football club based in Alcoy, a city located in the province of Alicante. The club was founded in 1928 and has a rich history in Spanish football.

Cd Alcoyano’s Stadium

Cd Alcoyano plays its home matches at the El Collao stadium, which has a capacity of approximately 4,850 spectators. The stadium has been the club’s home since its inauguration in 1929.

Cd Alcoyano’s Achievements

Over the years, Cd Alcoyano has achieved notable successes, including winning the Copa Federación de España in the 1991-1992 season. The team has also competed in various divisions of Spanish football, showcasing their talent and dedication.
