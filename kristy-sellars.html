Kristy Sellars (AGT) Bio, Age, Height, Husband, Wiki

Kristy Sellars is an Australian personality and has appeared many times on Australia’s Got Talent shows earlier in the past. This time, she appeared on the show- America’s Got Talent creating a buzz for the audience. Kristy showcases two talents on the stage of AGT- one is her pole dancing and the second is the art of animation.

First, on 17th June 2022, AGT released the video showing the pole dancing and a few glimpses of her animation during the auditions. Kristy is one of the finalists in the two-day grand finale of America’s Got Talent Season 17.

Kristy Sellars Bio, Age, Height, Parents, Husband, Net Worth

A native of Victoria, Australia, Kristy Sellars was born on 28th May 1986 and she is 33 years old. She is the mother of three kids along with being an entrepreneur (According to her Instagram profile). She received professional training in multiple dancing styles but thinks that pole dancing is something that makes her unique among all. It is her fourth appearance on the episode of 21st June 2022 on the AGT show including other appearances on Australia’s Got Talent.

Career

Since the release of Kristy’s performance on 21st June 2022, fans have been speculating who Kristy Sellars is and how she appeared to stay on the show last time. According to her social media profiles, she is the owner of multiple businesses including PhysiPole Studios, Enviro Grip, The Enviro Company, and Pole Scription.

AGT

Back in 2019, Kristy won Australia’s Got Talent show, where she got the chance to describe her art along with pole dancing on “The Pole Dance” podcast. During that time, she revealed a glimpse of her appearance and her style of pole dancing at Pole Theatre Sydney in 2016.

She says that she began experiencing and working with pole dancing along with animation art for the first time in 2013. Before beginning with her routine, Kristy explained that she belongs to Australia and is happily married along with being the mother of three kids.

Upon her arrival on the stage of the AGT show, the judges commented their thoughts on her type of act. The show revealed Kristy’s original and complete performance on Tuesday, 21st June 2022, where she got herself featured with the talent and skills of pole dancing.

Social Media

Despite being a famous personality because of her appearance on the various AGT shows, Kristy has over 20,800 followers on Instagram, around 21,000 followers on her Tik Tok account, and at least 14,000 subscribers on her YouTube account.

Most of her social media content is committed to pole dancing. Apart from pole dancing, she often posts videos and pictures or animated content on topics like money basics, how to mortgage, stock markets and their essentials, and many more.
