Zoe Sugg And Alfie Deyes Dating Life

Zoe Sugg and her partner Alfie Deyes have been dating since 2012 and live together.

As both of their content revolves heavily around showing aspects of their lives, they show up in their respective channels. 

Their respective social media also shows how close their relationship is. They celebrated their 10th anniversary a couple of months ago, with Alfie posting their celebration on his Instagram. 

They have a daughter, Ottilie Rue Deyes, who was born on August 29, 2021, and turned a year old earlier this year. Most of Zoe and Alfie's current content revolves around their daughter and their new lives as parents. They also dedicate much of their social media to little Ottilie.

They named their daughter Ottilie as it is the female version of the name Otto which means rich, prosperous, or wealthy. However, they both stated that they waited until the little girl was born to name her.
