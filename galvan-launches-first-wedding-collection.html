Galvan launches first wedding collection

Courtesy of Galvan

If you're a bride on the hunt for a simple and elegant wedding dress, then Galvan's latest move will prove very interesting for you. The fashion house has just launched its first bridal collection – and it is all about sleek gowns for the most modern of brides.

"We have worked hard to create contemporary yet timeless outfits so that our brides and bridesmaids will be as happy with their look today as they will be when looking back at their wedding photographs in 20 years’ time," co-founder and creative director, Sola Harrison told us.

Sitting aside the slightly more traditional lace and silk gowns, the collection also features jumpsuits, tailored trouser suits and more party-appropriate mini dresses.

Courtesy of Galvan

The range is particularly perfect for any brides that are planning on changing throughout their celebrations as there is truly something for every step of the day, including for those bridal brunches, walking down the aisle and even slinky evening frocks for dancing in.

"We know from our own experience that brides are no longer just looking for a gown," commercial director Katherine Holmgren said. "There’s a whole weekend of events to consider, with dress codes ranging from casual to formal, and this is how we’ve built our bridal collection. Versatile and clean, many of the styles can also be worn again for years to come."

The team's own weddings were truly a huge part of the inspiration behind the collection, design director Anna-Christin Haas told us, explaining that the very designs they wore feature in the range.

"Styles from our 25-piece bridal collection include the lace dresses that Katherine and I wore for our own wedding ceremonies, as well as several dresses I have designed for friends and family."

Prices for the pieces range from £895 for a mini dress to £5,995 for a lace gown. The 25-piece collection is already available to shop at GalvanLondon.com.
