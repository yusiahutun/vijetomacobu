List of all SGR stations from Nairobi to Mombasa (with pictures)

SGR is the best way to travel from Nairobi to Mombasa, thanks to Madaraka Express and Inter-County trains that run in both directions every day of the week. There are no stops in between if you choose the express train. However, if you opt for Inter-County train, it will stop at several SGR stations along the route. So, which are the SGR stations from Nairobi to Mombasa?

The Standard Gauge Railway (SGR) is a flagship project of the Kenyan government under Vision 2030 development agenda. It is the largest infrastructure project since the country attained independence in 1963. The project is aimed at simplifying transport operations between Nairobi and Mombasa. Plans are underway to expand the project to other towns in East Africa.

How many SGR stations are there? This is the biggest question you could be having at the back of your mind if you are planning to board an SGR train from Nairobi to Mombasa. There are nine SGR stations located along the route. According to kaributaxi.com, these are the SGR stations between Nairobi to Mombasa.

1. Nairobi Terminus (Syokimau)

This is the first of the SGR stations between Nairobi and Mombasa. The terminus is located in Syokimau suburb, south of Jomo Kenyatta International Airport. The distance from Nairobi CBD to Syokimau is approximately 18.8 km.

It is a 20-30 minutes drive by road from Nairobi CBD. However, if you travel by Nairobi commuter train, the journey will take approximately 40 minutes. The design of the SGR station features two trains with a bridge on top.

2. Athi River station

This is the second SGR station from Nairobi to Mombasa. It is located in Machakos County, approximately 20 km from Nairobi Terminus. This station serves the busy and vibrant town of Athi River and its environs.

3. Emali Station

This is the third SGR station along Nairobi - Mombasa SGR route. It is located in the fast-growing Emali Town in Makueni County, approximately 125 km from Nairobi. The station serves the residents of Emali Town and its environs.

4. Kibwezi Station

Kibwezi SGR station is one of the intermediate stations along the Nairobi - Mombasa SGR route. It is one of the towns where the Intercounty SGR train makes a scheduled stopover. The station is located in Kibwezi Town in Makueni County, approximately 178 km from Nairobi.

5. Mtito Andei station

This is one of the SGR stations in Kenya. It is located in Mtito Andei town in Makueni County. The SGR station is located approximately 230 km from Nairobi. This is the midway of the journey from Nairobi to Mombasa using the SGR.

6. Voi Station

The SGR station is located in Voi Town, the largest town in Taita-Taveta County. Voi is one of the most important commercial centres in the county. The town lies at the edge of the Tsavo National Park, which is a major tourist attraction in the country.

7. Miasenyi Station

The Miasenyi station is the sixth SGR station from Nairobi to Mombasa before reaching Mariakani. Its design features stripes, a signature beauty in the wild within the area where zebras are rampant.

8. Mariakani Station

This is the last SGR station before you reach Mombasa. It is located in the relatively small town of Mariakani, which lies on the boundary of the Kaloleni and Kinango districts. When you get to Mariakani en route to Mombasa, the long coconut trees give you the hope that you are about to reach your final destination.

9. Mombasa Terminus (Miritini)

This is the final destination if you are travelling from Nairobi to Mombasa. It is the second-largest SGR station after Nairobi. The SGR station is located in the Miritini suburb about 11 km from Mombasa CBD.

The design of the SGR station features concentric circles and a central tower representing a ripple in the ocean. At Miritini Mombasa, you will find buses, matatus, and taxis to take you around Mombasa.

Frequently asked questions about SGR stations from Nairobi to Mombasa

There are so many questions that you could be having about the SGR stations along the Nairobi-Mombasa route. Here are answers to some of the most frequently asked questions on this topic.

What time does SGR leave Nairobi to Mombasa?

The Inter-County Train leaves the Nairobi Terminus at 8.00 am and arrives in Mombasa at 2.00 pm. The afternoon Express train departs Nairobi Terminus at 3.00 pm and arrives in Mombasa at 8.00 pm. Alternatively, you can board the Night Express train that departs Nairobi Terminus at 10.00 pm and arrives in Mombasa at 3.35 am.

Where does SGR stop Mombasa?

There is only one SGR terminus in Mombasa, located in the Miritini suburb. However, you can board matatus and taxis to take you to other areas in Mombasa.

Where can I board SGR train in Nairobi?

You need to find your way to Syokimau, where the Nairobi SGR terminus is located. The journey takes between 20 and 40 minutes from Nairobi CBD, depending on the mode of transport.
