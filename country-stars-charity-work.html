15 Country Stars Who Have Gone Above and Beyond for Charity

Country stars have been known to go above and beyond for a good cause. Beyond simply donating to a charity or performing at a benefit, a number of artists have adopted a charitable cause as their own, or even founded a non-profit for a worthy cause; in fact, it may surprise you just how much good one of your favorite artist has done.

Did you know Keith Urban once canceled a concert and showed up to a fan's hospice center to meet and serenade her before she died? Or that Kimberly and Brad Paisley are creating a food bank for the needy in Nashville? From Dolly Parton giving 100 million books to children to Tim McGraw and Faith Hill springing into action when disasters strike, all of the country superstars in this gallery have done their share of good deeds.
