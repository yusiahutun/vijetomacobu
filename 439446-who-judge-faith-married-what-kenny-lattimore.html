Who is Kenny Lattimore?

Kenny was born on April 10, 1970, in Washington DC. He is 52 years old as of 2022. The name of his father is Walter Lattimore, and his mother is Sonya Ball Ryan. Her mother worked as a counsellor at Washington's Howard University before passing away in 1988. He grew up alongside his two siblings, Audrey Lattimore and Trinita Lattimore.

Lattimore enrolled in music classes in high school, where he studied classics and learnt to read musical notation. When he enrolled at Howard, he planned to study architecture and urban planning. However, it didn't take long for him to be drawn back into music. Before his freshman year was over, he had joined a group called Mannequin.

Unfortunately, Mannequin broke up in 1990, but he didn't give up his music career. He signed with Columbia Records in late 1994 and released his debut album, Kenny Lattimore, in 1996. Since then, he has released several studio albums, including:
