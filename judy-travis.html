Career

Judy Travis is active on social sites since her early days. She posted a how-to-hair video on her YouTube channel which was a smashing hit that encouraged her to upload more videos. In 2014, she signed with Driver Digital to represent “ItsJudysLife” and “Its JudyTime”. She has two other YouTube channels, one of which is a video blog channel and the other is a Spanish-language beauty channel. Her most popular videos are tutorials based on Selena Gomez's looks in her music videos.
