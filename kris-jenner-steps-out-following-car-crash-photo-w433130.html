Kris Jenner Steps Out Following Car Crash: Photo

She’s still smiling! Kris Jenner appeared to be in good spirits as she stepped out in Los Angeles on Friday, August 5, just two days after she was involved in a car accident.

The Keeping Up With the Kardashians star, 60, looked gorgeous in a black pantsuit as she stepped out for a lunch date with her four eldest daughters, Kourtney, Kim and Khloé Kardashian, and Kendall Jenner.

The Kardashian-Jenner clan met on Friday afternoon to have lunch with the families of gun-violence victims. Kim, 35, later shared a shot of the group via Instagram, writing, “Having lunch with the families of loved ones who were killed by gun violence. I learned a lot from listening to their stories. Life is so precious! What will it take for this to stop? #NotOneMore #Everytown #MomsDemand.”

Kris notably arrived at the luncheon in her black Range Rover, since she crashed her one-week-old $250,000 white Rolls-Royce earlier in the week.

As Us Weekly previously reported, the momager was driving in Calabasas, California, on Wednesday, when another vehicle cut in front of her and collided with her car, badly damaging the entire front end of the luxury vehicle. A local sergeant later told Us that Kris was treated for injuries at the scene.

Kris’ youngest daughter, Kylie Jenner, took to Twitter moments after the news of the car accident broke, telling her 17.3 million followers she “was not involved in the accident” and that “everything’s ok.”
