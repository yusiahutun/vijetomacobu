Mr Godfrey said that a camcorder and tapes were found during a police search of the flat where Newis had been living in December 2018.

Mr Godfrey said: "They follow that pattern throughout. It would seem that he was quite infatuated with her and was making lots of offers. It was him asking for these photos and her sending them."

Newis had a previous conviction for harassment and a caution but he had not been in trouble since 2018.
