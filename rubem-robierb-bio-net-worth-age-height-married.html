Rubem Robierb Bio, Net Worth, Age, Height &amp; Married

Rubem is a Brazilian visual artist who rose to fame after marrying an American weather anchor and television show host, Sam Champion. He is known as one of the finest art photographer throughout the world winning the hearts of millions with his phenomenal artistry.

With nine honorable mentions at the 2012 International Photography Awards, Rubem’s photography and awesome art were recognized all over the globe. Followingly, he has amassed a hefty net worth. Here is all the information about Rubem Robierb you must have wondered about.

Who is Rubem Robierb?

Rubem Robierb was born on December 1, 1976, in Bacabal, Maranhao, Brazil. Rubem Robierb began writing poetry since a young age, but he soon developed an interest in photography and how images can affect the viewer in an emotional sense.

The fine visual artist moved to São Paulo at the age of twenty with the intention of attending the school of photography. Later, he started his commercial photography for the advertising industry and as an artist. Interested in lifestyle, he is always keen to capture people, ethnicities, and scenery.

Rubem Robierb’s Career

Robierb’s work caught the attention of the Art et Partage Association, which commissioned his first solo exhibition in Aix-en-Provence, France, in 2005, Brazil Autrement (Otherwise Brazil). Subsequently, his photographs were exhibited in the galleries of São Paulo, Zurich, Monaco, Paris, and Milan.

Moreover, he opened a Wynwood studio and started to participate in Miami’s artistic atmosphere with a constant presence in Art Basel week.

Robierb took part in group shows such as Corpo Revelado, Blue Life Cultural Space, São Paulo, Brazil, Art Curial for Fighting Aids Monaco Foundation, Monaco, Photophantasma, Avant Gallery, Miami, Arteaméricas Art Fair, Canal Gallery & Miami.
