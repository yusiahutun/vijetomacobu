Billy Porter Wears Christian Siriano Tuxedo Gown to Oscars 2019

  • Billy Porter arrived to the Oscars red carpet in a tuxedo dress by Christian Siriano.
  • The Pose star is hosting red carpet coverage for the awards ceremony for ABC tonight.
  • Porter previously stunned on the red carpet at the Golden Globes in a custom cape and suit by New York-based designer Randi Rahm.

Billy Porter isn't playing around on the Oscars red carpet tonight. The Pose star arrived ready to snatch wigs in an actual tuxedo gown by Christian Siriano, which looked worthy of its own Academy Award.

The velvety look featured silk lapels, white ruffled sleeves, a black bow tie, and a voluminous ball skirt. To say Porter slayed in the ensemble is an understatement.
