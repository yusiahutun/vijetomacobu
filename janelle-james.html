Janelle James is an American comedian, writer, and actress. Her writing credits include Black Monday, and she acted on the programs Crashing, Corporate, and Central Park. Her debut comedy album Black and Mild was released in 2017. She was named one of Variety's 10 Comics to Watch (2020). James currently portrays principal Ava Coleman on the ABC series Abbott Elementary, for which she received a 2022 TCA Award nomination for Individual Achievement in Comedy.
