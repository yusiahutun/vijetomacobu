Say goodbye to dry cleaning

Made exclusively with Zegna’s innovative TECHMERINOTM fabric treated with special finishing techniques to ensure maximum comfort and perfect thermo-regulation, this suit is incredibly machine washable.

Simply fold the suit according to instructions to protect the canvas of the jacket, put it in the dedicated wash case provided and place in a washing machine with 400 turns or less. Gently wash at a temperature of 30°C, drip-dry overnight, and hey presto, you have a clean and crisp suit to wear in the morning. Should you wish the suit to look fresher and more formal, light ironing will do the trick. Maintaining a suit has never been easier.

Noble fibre goes high-tech

The technology behind the machine washable suit is nothing short of a marvel. Made from pure biodegradable Australian Merino wool obtained with a controlled, sustainable production process certified by The Woolmark Company, TECHMERINOTM garments guarantee excellent technical performance in full respect of the environment, while offering greater resistance to wear.

What’s more, the highly breathable fabric comes with excellent hygroscopic properties to keep your body temperature constant and your skin to stay dry even on a steamy summer day. The natural lightness, softness and elasticity of the fabric deliver a constant fit for easy manoeuvring even in the most dynamic of situations that you can find yourself in.

Chic and streamlined style

Constructed in a slim-fitting, soft-shouldered silhouette, the two-piece is composed of a smart two-button single-breasted jacket with a thin invisible lining to provide support while maintaining its ultra lightness, and slim-cut flat-front trousers with button fastening but a drawstring waistband at the back. The sleek suit with a naturally casual fit comes in navy and light blue and is best worn with a pair of luxe sneakers for a cool city look.
