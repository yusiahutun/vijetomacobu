The Flash actor Rick Cosnett comes out: 'I made a promise to myself to live my truth everyday'

Rick Cosnett of The Flash has come out as gay.

The 36-year-old actor opened up about his orientation with a candid Instagram video on Friday, Valentine's Day.

'Hi everyone. Dramatic pause…I'm gay,' he told his 226k followers.
