Bright fans counting down days until new music video after 'freaking hot' photoshoot

Thai star Bright Vachirawit has been reigning the global trends. From his effortless fashion to his brilliant acting, Bright has won the hearts of millions with his multitude of talents and now fans wait for his latest track Try which will be released soon.

Bright also became the first global ambassador of Burberry from the South Asia-Pacific region and often dons heartthrob looks for the luxury brand making fans swoon over him all over again. Recently, he sported a casual white t-shirt and black stripe trousers from the brand’s latest collection and fans agreed, “freaking hot.”

Bright fans counting down days until new music video

Along with acting, fans can never get over the soulful voice of Bright Vachirawit and the Thai star is coming up with a new song Try featuring Thai-Swiss artist Matcha. The song will be released worldwide on August 18 and the teaser of the song already promises another masterpiece.

In the short teaser of Try, Bright appears in all black with long hair, and the close-up of his face reflects the mood of the song which will be heart-wrenching. Matcha looks stunning in a bold black dress with a piercing gaze. The verdict of the song is out as fans are counting down the hours for another “blast.”

Bright is serving the ultimate ‘boyfriend’ look in latest Burberry shoot

Bright’s fashion merges beautifully with the spirit of Burberry and as a result, fans get jaw-dropping shots of the Thai hero. From his airport looks in Burberry sporting all black to him casually posing on a ‘Sunny day,’ there’s no doubt that Bright can pull off a Burberry like no other.

What’s next for Bright Vachirawit?

While television has been Bright’s breakthrough to global stardom, the 2gether star is now aiming for the big screen as fans wait eagerly for his 2024 film The Interest paired opposite to award-winning actress Urassaya Sperbund, in short Yaya.

The Interest is an official remake of Han Dong Wook’s 2014 South Korean film Man in Love where a low-level debt collector comes across a woman struggling to take care of her father with huge debt over her head. Eventually, he falls for her only to know that he has a terminal illness.
