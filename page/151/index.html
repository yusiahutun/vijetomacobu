Live Nation Announces Lawn Pass Program For 2019

published on July 19, 2024
LOS ANGELES, (CelebrityAccess) — Live Nation has revived their popular ‘season pass’ program with the launch of the Live Nation Lawn Pass. The pass provides unlimited lawn admission to all concerts at any participating Live Nation amphitheater in the U.S. throughout the 2019 concert season for one price. The passes will go on-sale on December 4th and will be available through December 18th, or until they run out.
