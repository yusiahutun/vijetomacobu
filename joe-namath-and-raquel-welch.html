About

Joe Namath is a 80 year old American Football (American) Player. Born Joseph William Namath on 31st May, 1943 in Beaver Falls, Pennsylvania, USA, he is famous for Hall of Fame quarterback for the New York Jets. His zodiac sign is Gemini.

American Actress Raquel Welch was born Jo Raquel Tejada on 5th September, 1940 in Chicago, Illinois, USA and passed away on 15th Feb 2023 Los Angeles, California, USA aged 82. She is most remembered for her roles as Cora Peterson in the 1966 science fiction adventure film Fantastic Voyage, Loana in the 1966 adventure fantasy film One Million Years B.C., Fathom Harvill in the 1967 spy comedy film Fathom, Lust/Lilian Lust in the 1967 comedy DeLuxe Color film Bedazzled, Maria Stoner in the 1968 Western film Bandolero!, Myra Breckinridge in the 1970 comedy film Myra Breckinridge, and Constance Bonacieux in the 1973 swashbuckler film The Three Musketeers and its 1974 sequel film The Four Musketeers. Her zodiac sign is Virgo.
