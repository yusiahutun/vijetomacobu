Russell Neal - Bio, Age, Career, Height, Net Worth, Facts

Russell Neal is a well-known singer, songwriter, musician, and social media influencer from the United States. Russell Neal is popular for being a member of the popular and famous R&B band “Hi-Five.”

Early Life

On the 29th of May 1974, Russell Neal was born in Waco, Texas, in the United States of America. He holds an American nationality and is 49 years old as of 2023. In addition, he has his belief in Christianity religion and belongs to the African-American ethnicity.

He was born to his father Neal II, and his Mother Dr.Evelyn T. Neal. Professionally, his father was a lawyer while his mother was a doctor. Moreover, he grew up with his two brothers, Rodney Neal, and Ronald Neal.

Talking about his academic qualifications, he went to a local high school and completed his schooling. Later, he went to California University and earned a bachelor’s degree there.

Professional Career

Hi-Five

Talking about his musical career, Russell Neal was passionate about singing from a young age. Moreover, he was one of the established and original members of the platinum-selling R&B music band Hi-Five.

The band was established in the year 1989, along with his fellow friends, Tony Thompson as a Lead Vocalist, and Marcus Sanders, Tristan Irby, Shannon Gill, and Tarrell Carr as Vocals.

Caption: The Hi-Five band (Source: The Hollywood Reporter)

In the year 1990, he became popular, after the group scored Top 10 hits such as “I Like the Way,” even appearing on BET, Arsenio Hall, and “Soul Train. Moreover, the band also scored a top hit on RCA records in 1991 “I Like the Way (The Kissing Game).”

Russell, who was 16 at the time, was at the top of his game, and according to Jonathan Kinloch, the group’s road manager, Russell was referred to as “Cool Russ.” He was endorsed as the dreamy one with the smooth moves labeled as a “Lovelorn Lothario.”

At the last of the year 1992, he felt Hi-Five was stagnated while he needed to have more of a singing role, something contrary to the labels, and the producer didn’t think that that was an appropriate decision so he left.

Relationship Status

Allegation of murdering his wife

Russell made headlines in June 2014 when he was arrested and charged with the murder of his wife, Catherin. According to reports, Russell went to the police two days after killing his wife. Their kids were locked up in a different room in the same house at the time. As a result, Russell was eventually arrested and charged with first-degree murder.

Despite this, Russell was released on bail, which was revoked about a month later. While in jail, he began referring to himself as Jesus Christ. Prosecutors claimed he began “playing crazy” and was found unfit to stand trial.

Since then, Russell has been admitted to Rusk State Hospital in Texas, despite authorities’ claims that he knew what he was doing when he attacked his wife in 2014.

Russell Neal is currently being evaluated while awaiting trial, and he claims he was temporarily insane at the time of the alleged murder. Despite this, the charges remain, and Russell will almost certainly face a legal battle in the future.
