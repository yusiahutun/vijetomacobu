The 15 Best Designer Fanny Packs

Sometimes the simplest designs are the ones worth investing in. Il Bisonte's belt bag is made from smooth, cowhide leather, that has been tanned and responsibly produced in Italy. The small, rounded pouch hangs perfectly on the leather belt. Choose from multiple colors, from a neutral black or caramel, to something with a little more oomph, like this ruby red.

Size: 33, 35, 37" belt lengths

Material: Cowhide Leather

Color: Ruby Red, Black, Milk, Caramel, Natural, Wisteria
