Kevin Costners Wife Christine Baumgartner: Age, Net Worth, Wiki

The model turned handbag designer Christine Baumgartner is famous as the wife of veteran actor Kevin Costner. She had already started in the industry long before getting married to the actor.

Baumgartner has done some work as a model and actress in various movies and shows like Primetime (1989), Die Johannes B. Kerner Show (1998), and E! Live from the Red Carpet (1995). She later got to know about her true passion -handbags and followed it.

Here we get to know more about Christine Baumgartner- Kevin Costner’s wife, including her age, net worth, and wiki.

Life History

Christine Anne Baumgartner was born on May 4 in the year 1974. She is now (2022) 47 years old. She is from Los Angeles, California, with German and Swiss background.

Christine’s father is Jim Baumgartner, whereas there is no information about her mother. She has a sister Lily Baumgartner. Christine is quite smart and has done her undergraduate studies in Business at California State University, Fullerton. She was also doing modeling part-time along with her studies.

Love life with Kevin Costner

Kevin and his wife, Christine, met in the ’80s when Kevin was still married to Cindy Costner. They first met at a golf course. The couple has 20 years age difference. Costner and his spouse, Baumgartner, were colleagues as Christine worked as a professional model.

After a while in 1999, they crossed their ways in a restaurant where Kevin asked for Christine’s number. He told her that he would call in a week or so. They started seeing each other and later fell in love.

Kevin Costner smiling with his wife, Christine Baumgartner

In 2003, Christine and Kevin had a minor setback as a couple. Kevin was not so sure about wanting kids and having a family, but Christine surely wanted one. Kevin thought that he would not be a good dad and wouldn’t be able to see his children grow as he was getting old.

However, Christine told Kevin that she would wait for him but not for long. Later, Kevin decided that he didn’t want to throw such a good relationship and person for a silly reason, and they got back together.

On 2004, September 25, after ups and downs in their relationship, Costner and Baumgartner got married. They held the ceremony in Aspen, Colorado, in their house with 400 guests. The couple made a magnificent entrance at their wedding. Kevin entered in a vintage green vehicle, whereas Christine entered in a horse-drawn carriage. It was a fairytale come true.

Children

Although having thoughts about starting a family, Kevin and his partner, Christine, welcomed their first child in 2007. They named their first son Cayden Wyatt Costner (May 6, 2007). Wanting to have a bigger family, the couple welcomed two more children. The middle child Hayes Logan Costner (February 12, 2009), and the youngest, Grace Avery Costner (June 2, 2010).

The happy couple still lives together and raises their children out of the limelight. The family sometimes makes appearances in different movie premieres and red carpet.

Net Worth

Kevin’s wife started her career as a model but later chose to design stylish carrying handbags. She started the business with her friend Tamara and named it Cat Bag Couture. The business name is an anagram of Christine’s and Tamara’s names. Some of the daytime purses from Cat Bag Couture can be seen in the pilot episode in 2005 of Desperate Housewives.

Christine mentions that Kevin was immensely supportive of her business. Kevin being famous also helped to uplift her selling. The handbags can be shopped online and in Boutiques in Aspen.

As an owner of a successful business, Christine’s net worth sums up to be $5 million (2022).

She has not been spotted on any social media sites.
