Who Is Sofa Sisniega? Facts To Know About The Actress

Mexican actress Sofia Sisniega was raised by her beloved parents, Marcel Sisniega Campbell (father) and Patricia Aspe (mother). Find out her net worth in the below article.

Sofia Sisniega is an aspiring & versatile Mexican actress who is popular internationally, thanks to her portrayal of Sofía López-Haro in the tv series Gossip Girl: Acapulco. Active since 2006, Sisniega has worked in numerous hit films.

Besides, Sofia is also known for appearing in Here on Earth, Crazy for Change, Club de Cuervos, and Compulsion. From an early age, Sisniega developed an interest in acting.

Apart from her work as an actress, Sofia is an animal-rights activist and vegetarian. So, in her spare time, Sisniega rescues animals and assists with setting them up for adoption.

Who Is Sofía Sisniega? Wikipedia Biography

Sofia Sisniega is a prominent Mexican actress whose Wikipedia bio says she began acting in local community theatre productions just at the age of 4. And she debuted on the big screen by landing a role in Mujeres en el Acto in 2006.

Her notable television roles can be seen in series such as Los Minondo, Bienvenida Realidad, Gossip Girl: Acapulco, Sr. Ávila, etc. And Gossip Girl: Acapulco is considered the turning point of her career.

Besides, Sofia was accepted in the Conservatory at the Stella Adler Studio of Acting in New York City. There she studied for three years before graduating. Likewise, she took acting classes at Casa Azul and Centro Universitario de Teatro (CUT) in Mexico City.

What Is Sofia Sisniega Age? Her Parents Details

Born on June 7, 1989, Sofia Sisniega’s age is 32 years now. She was raised by her parents, Marcel Sisniega Campbell and Patricia Aspe. Meanwhile, she is a native of Cuernavaca, Mexico.
