Jedediah Bila Timeline With Partner! How Boyfriend Rose As A Beaming Husband

American political commentator Jedediah Bilas' Valentine's of 2018 had a special feel to it. Not only did she enjoyed the whole month of February to the fullest as she also made a big decision about her personal life. 

The New York native is a television host, author, and columnist. She is best known as the former co-host of talk show The View, aired on ABC network. A strong libertarian in political beliefs, she is a regular presence in the TV screens having appeared in FOX, CNBC, MSNBC and Lifetime as a guest contributor.

Back On The View: Jedediah Bila makes a guest appearance on The View (Published on 18 October 2018)

She is also the author of two books Outnumbered: Chronicles of a Manhattan Conservative (2011) and DoNotDisturb (2018). She was nominated twice for the Daytime Emmy Award in the year 2017 and 2018.

As a columnist, she has written for several news outlets including HumanEvents.com, DailyCaller.com, NewsMax.com, and AMAC. 

Jedediah Bila's Bio: Ethnicity, Education

Jedediah was born as A. Jedediah Louisa Bila on 29 January 1979 in Brooklyn, New York. She possesses a mixed ethnicity as her parents were of Italian Jewish descent. Since her early age, she was exposed to political conversation amongst her family, which developed the capacity to debate and form her own opinions.

She graduated from Wagner College with a Bachelor's degree in Spanish in 2000. Later she received Master's degree in Spanish Literature from the Columbia University. After the graduation, she served as a teacher, student advisor, and academic dean at the Birch Wathen Lenox School in Manhattan.

Her Career: Appearances In Various Television Channels

Jedediah made a television debut appearing as a guest on Fox News's political talk show, Hannity in 2011. She joined Fox News in 2013, where she served as a co-host and panelist on its various shows such as Outnumbered and The Five.

Interview time: Jedediah Bila questions  American politician and diplomat Hilary Clinton on ABC's The View in September 2017 (Photo: Youtube.com)

Jedediah shocked the viewers when she announced her departure from The View in September 2017. However, she did not reveal the root of her departure.

But on 17 October 2018, she made a return guest appearance on The View, The Greg Gulfielf Show and co-hosted The Five and appeared on several Sirius XM shows and surprised the audience. 

She Had Liberal Boyfriends In The Past

After being placed as a co-host of The View, she revealed about her past boyfriends in the show. 

In her interview with TV Insider in November 2016, she revealed that the best part of being on The View was she could talk about a variety of topics including her personal life, not just politics.

Married Long-term Boyfriend After Eight Months Of Engagement

Jedediah is a married woman, who shares the conjugal bond with her long-term boyfriend Jeremy Scher. Jeremy is a graduate of the Rice University, who serves as a corporate strategist at Hachette Book Group.

The couple had been engaged in July 2017. The outspoken pundit Instagrammed a picture of her husband-to-be kneeling in front of her and wrote: "she said yes." Not just that, she had also flaunted her diamond engagement ring her show, The View on multiple occasions.

The libertarian pundit and her partner shared the wedding vow at Huntington, New York on 17 February 2018, 'the month of valentines' after eight months of engagement. The wedding ceremony was witnessed by 60 of their closest family and friends.

Partners For Life: Jedediah poses with her husband, Jeremy in February 2018 (Photo: Usmagazine.com)

As any girl would be, Jedediah too was excited and was on cloud nine for her wedding with the love of her life. In an interview with e-online, a day before their wedding, she expressed her excitement saying:

"I’m super excited. Super, super excited! I always wanted to get married, but I never thought I would do the big wedding dress and the beautiful venue. I always thought I was gonna be on a beach somewhere, just a couple of friends, doing it really casual. So it’s crazy."
