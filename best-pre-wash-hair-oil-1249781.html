    How to Choose the Best Pre-wash Hair Oil?

    Here are a few aspects that you should consider before splurging on the best pre-wash hair oil - 

    Hair Type: Before you vouch for some pre-shampoo oils, make sure you analyze your hair type thoroughly. There are oils that are specifically crafted to improve dry, damaged, and brittle hair, whereas some are formulated for chemically, keratin, and color-treated hair. In addition, you will also find oils that act as hair and scalp treatments to combat dandruff and itchy scalp. Hence, do your homework wisely.

    Type of Hair Oil: Make sure you pay heed to the type of hair oil you are planning to invest in. Check whether the oil is meant for your hair type, hair texture, and scalp condition. You will come across pre-wash oil treatment, prewash scalp oil, as well as oil that can be used on skin and nails. Depending upon your needs and necessities, get your hands on the best and most suitable oil that will help you embrace healthier hair. 

    Ingredients: Ensure that you go through the list of ingredients mentioned on the packaging of the pre-shampoo oil. It is wise to invest in oils that are made from a natural, organic, pure, and botanical blend of ingredients like argan oil, avocado oil, jojoba oil, castor oil, olive oil, biotin, vitamin E, meadowfoam seed oil, bamboo extract, holistic plant roots, moringa oil, sunflower oil, etc. Stay away from oils that contain harsh chemicals and additives. 

    Formula: Always pick the best pre-shampoo hair oil that is vegan, cruelty-free, hypoallergenic, and free from toxins. Avoid oils that are made from phthalates, sulfates, gluten, and silicon. On the other hand, make sure that the oil is light in weight and features a non-greasy and non-sticky texture.
