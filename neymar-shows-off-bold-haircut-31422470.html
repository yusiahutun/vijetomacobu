Neymar shows off bold new haircut as Brazil star continues injury rehab

Brazilian superstar Neymar has showed off his bold new look as he continues his treatment after a recent injury.

Neymar suffered a knee injury while playing for Brazil over the international break - and it looks to set to rule him out for the rest of the Saudi Pro League season. However, he is still making headlines away from the pitch, this time with his haircut.

The 31-year-old shared a snap on his Instagram story of his hair looking almost all buzzed off and his beard shaved, leaving just a moustache. He captioned the snap: "New look!"
