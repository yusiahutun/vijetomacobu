Kaye Adams put on a very animated display as she jumped in the air to celebrate turning 60 on Wednesday.

'As a life-long age-phobic who has spent the past nine months navel-gazing with Karen Mackenzie on our HTB60 podcast, the short answer is that it feels much the same as yesterday.

The presenter added: 'But speaking to so many fellow ‘60 clubbers’ on the podcast has really made me think about how I want to approach however many years I have ahead and, to boil it down to a single line, I want to spend less time focused on climbing and more time focused on growing. 

'I am a typical Capricorn, a little mountain goat who just gets its head down and keeps trekking on up towards the summit.

'Now I want to look around a little bit and take in the sights, maybe even take a diversion or two or three. Still a work in progress but inspired by all the emails we’ve received on HTB60 from people doing just that and loving life. Everything is on the table apart from one red line.

'@karen.mackenzie.92 …I’ve learned a lot from you but I am never, ever going to start doing jigsaws.'

Dance move: Kaye even received a cake with a model of Kaye twerking on the top

Kaye celebrated her 60th birthday with Loose Women stars Coleen Nolan, Denise Welch, Linda Robson and Kelle Bryan.

They surprised her live on air with funny gifts, champagne, balloons and a cake with a model of Kaye twerking on the top. 

Kaye revealed she lied to her children and admitted they thought she was 10 years younger than she was.

She said: ‘Yeh they did. I did. I feel quite ashamed of it now actually. It was one of those things that developed over the years. I was an older mum and at the school gates with younger mum.

'I had to confess to my daughter that mummy was 10 years older than she thought. She was so upset. She looked crestfallen. I kept lying to the rest of the world. My mum was the same. I know it’s bonkers and this year I decided to embrace it’
