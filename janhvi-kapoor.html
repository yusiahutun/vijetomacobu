Janhvi Kapoor is an Indian actress who primarily works in Bollywood. She was born on March 6, 1997, in Mumbai, India, to famous Bollywood actress Sridevi and film producer Boney Kapoor. After completing her education, Janhvi pursued a career in acting and made her Bollywood debut in 2018 with the film Dhadak, which was a commercial success. Since then, she has acted in several Bollywood films, including Ghost Stories, Gunjan Saxena: The Kargil Girl, Roohi, and Dostana 2. Janhvi is known for her natural acting skills and her ability to portray a wide range of emotions on screen. She has won several awards for her performances, including the Zee Cine Award for Best Female Debut in 2019.
