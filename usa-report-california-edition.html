'Innovation horsepower' at the core of D&amp;K Engineering

Developing products from concept to reality, D&K Engineering breeds industry leaders by helping companies bring innovative products to the market. Hiring the best engineers and having world-class processes, equipment and tools under one roof, D&K Engineering designs and manufactures breakthrough hardware-based products that lead to profitable and enduring lines of business.

"The innovation horsepower is at our core," says CEO Scott Dennis, who co-founded the company with Alex Kunczynski in 1999. "The continuous interaction between our research and development (R&D) and manufacturing teams allows us to shrink time to market, reduce costs and improve the quality of results for companies." 

A global leader in end-to-end product realisation services focused on leading-edge content, D&K Engineering serves a variety of sectors - from robotics, electromechanical and medical devices to 3D printing and smart machines. Having developed more than 200 products since its foundation, D&K Engineering serves a diverse mix of Fortune 500 companies, middle capitalisation firms and start-ups.

"Serving multiple market segments helps us apply business techniques in adjacent markets," Dennis says. "As a manufacturer, we help clients create innovative designs and products, but as a partner we also help them create a strategy for success by delivering advanced solutions and services that solve real problems."

From its humble beginnings as a top-tier product development and manufacturing company in San Diego, California, D&K Engineering has grown to become an agile international player, nurturing cooperation with the world's largest suppliers and R&D firms. Its global supply chain network extends to Asia with its Singapore office managing the company's customer and supplier relationships in the region. The Singapore base also mirrors the company's R&D, prototyping and manufacturing activities in its headquarters in the United States.

With partnerships becoming the natural order in an increasingly knowledge-based global economy, D&K Engineering welcomes more alliances particularly with well-established suppliers in Malaysia, as it moves towards areas where innovation organically flows.
