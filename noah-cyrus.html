Noah Cyrus News, Music, Photos And Videos Hollywood Life

Bio:
Noah Cyrus (born Noah Lindsey Cyrus on January 8, 2000 in Nashville, Tennessee) is an American actress and singer. Born to singer Billy Ray Cyrus and pretty lady Tish Cyrus, Noah was bitten by the performance bug at an early age. She appeared on several episodes of her dad’s show Doc from 2003 to 2004, then made several guest appearances on sister Miley Cyrus‘ show Hannah Montana from 2006 to 2010. She has also dabbled in music, recording the theme song for her 2008 movie Ponyo with Frankie Jonas. She released her debut single “Make Me (Cry)” in 2016, and has since put out songs like “Stay Together” and “I’m Stuck.” Her debut album is due out in 2018. She has been nominated for awards at events like the Radio Disney Music Awards, MTV Video Music Awards and Billboard Music Awards.

Best Known For:
Noah is best known for being the younger sister of Miley Cyrus.

Personal Life:
Noah confirmed her relationship with rapper Lil Xan in July 2018.
