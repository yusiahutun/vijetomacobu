A Look at Victoria Beckham's 15 Engagement Rings

Victoria Beckham and David Beckham have been one of the most fashion-forward couples since they first got together more than two decades ago, so it's no surprise that the designer's collection of engagement rings is one for the ages.

When David Beckham first proposed to the then-Spice Girls singer in 1998, he did so with a three-carat marquise-cut diamond, which the couple proudly showed off outside their hotel room for photographers.

Since tying the knot on July 4, 1999, Victoria has added to her jewelry collection, sporting many different sparklers on her ring finger over the years.

Beckham has shown off 15 rings total since that day, in all colors, shapes and sizes, and they're all just as posh as the next. (And though we haven't confirmed the exact makeup of each gem, for the purposes of this slideshow we'll assume they're real precious stones.)

Take a look at her gorgeous engagement ring collection ahead.

1998: Victoria Beckham’s marquise-cut diamond ring

To pop the question, David presented Victoria with a three-carat marquise-cut diamond on a plain yellow gold band. The ring was reported to cost around $85,000 at the time.

On Jan. 25, 1998, Beckham showed off her ring as she and David announced their engagement to the world outside their hotel in Chester.

Ahead of the couple's nuptials in 1999, Beckham continued to wear the ring at various events, including a few Spice Girls appearances.

2001: Victoria Beckham’s eternity band

Around 2001, Beckham appeared to swap out her original engagement ring for an eternity band that featured white diamonds. She gave a closer glimpse of the new ring during a book signing of her autobiography, Learning to Fly, in October 2001.

She continued to wear the ring for over a year before moving on to her next piece of jewelry.

2003: Victoria Beckham’s emerald-cut diamond ring with side baguettes

In May 2003, Beckham and her husband wowed at the MTV Movie Awards as they donned matching white ensembles. However, it was Beckham's stunning ring that captured everyone's attention. The former Spice Girl donned a huge emerald-cut diamond with side baguettes as she walked the red carpet.

Later that year, she was seen in the ring again, as Beckham received the OBE (Officer of the Order of the British Empire) from Queen Elizabeth II at Buckingham Palace in November 2003. 

2004: Victoria Beckham’s pink oval-cut diamond ring

By the time 2004 rolled around, Beckham had swapped out her ring again, giving a closer glimpse of the new sparkler as she attended the 19 Management 19Th Anniversary Party at the Royal Albert Hall alongside David.

As she rested her hand on David's back, photographers got a glimpse of a new, oval-cut pink diamond ring. David reportedly gifted Beckham the ring for her 30th birthday, which was worth $1.1 million at the time, per Vogue.

2005: Victoria Beckham’s pear-cut diamond ring

Beckham didn't stick with the same ring for long. Though she did sport the pink diamond throughout 2004, she showed off a new ring as she attended The David Beckham Academy launch party with her husband in June 2005.

The pear-cut ring was said to be 17 carats and featured a diamond pave band. Beckham certainly didn't let the stunning ring go to waste as she donned it at many events in 2005, including Milan Fashion Week, Swarovski Fashion Rocks for the Prince's Trust and notably Elton John and David Furnish's civil partnership ceremony in December 2005.

2006: Victoria Beckham’s emerald-cut yellow diamond ring

In 2006, Beckham paired her sleek new bob with a brand-new ring. Beckham was first seen wearing the new ring, featuring a yellow emerald-cut diamond, around June 2006 as she cheered on David during the World Cup.

She gave a better glimpse of the ring as she attended New York Fashion Week in September 2006, putting her jewel front and center as she attended the Marc Jacobs show.

2007: Victoria Beckham’s cushion-cut emerald ring

In 2007, Beckham began to experiment with gemstones as she donned a cushion-cut emerald ring. She gave photographers a peek at the ring as she attended the annual Elton John AIDS Foundation Oscar Party in Februray 2007, pairing the sparkler with a matching emerald bracelet on her right wrist.

She was seen wearing the ring on a few other occasions that year, including the Sport Industry Awards with David.

2008: Victoria Beckham’s emerald-cut diamond ring

While Beckham's hair got shorter in 2008, her ring size certainly didn't shrink. Early 2008 saw the star donning the massive pear-shaped diamond, but she swapped it out for an emerald-cut diamond ring later in the year.

She gave a good look at the ring during New York Fashion Week in September 2008, as she attended the Marc Jacobs fashion show. Pulling the whole look together, she paired the ring with equally shimmery earrings.

2009: Victoria Beckham’s oval-cut ruby ring

Beckham rotated through multiple rings in her collection in 2009, including her cushion-cut emerald ring during Milan Fashion Week and her pear-cut diamond ring during an Armani store opening in New York City.

But in keeping with her tradition, In May 2009, she debuted a new ring as she attended the Met Gala. The oval-cut ruby ring was a striking complement to her one-shoulder polka dot dress for the "Model as Muse" theme.

2009: Victoria Beckham’s oval-cut sapphire ring

Rubies are red, and later that year, Beckham decided she liked blue. Towards the end of 2009, she debuted a new ring on her left finger, an oval-cut sapphire ring.

She was seen wearing the jewel at the British Fashion Awards in December 2009, and again during various appearances in 2010, including the Vanity Fair Oscar Party where she put it on full display as she tucked her hair behind her ear.

2010: Victoria Beckham’s pink oval-cut diamond ring

In February 2010, Beckham went with something more dainty on her ring finger: An oval-cut pink diamond ring, worn to the Bergdorf Goodman launch of her new collection, Victoria Beckham Denim. Unlike her pink diamond from 2004, this one featured a yellow-gold pave band.

2015: Victoria Beckham’s round-cut diamond ring

Over the next five years, Beckham continued to recycle her previous rings, until 2015 when she debuted a round-cut diamond ring that had a halo setting and round side stones. She only wore it for a handful of appearances, including the 2015 amfAR Hong Kong Gala in March, before moving on to her next new piece.

2015: Victoria Beckham’s moonstone ring

Just a few months later, Beckham debuted a unique moonstone on her ring finger as she attended the 2015 CFDA Fashion Awards in June. So as not to take away from the ring, she went for an all-black look with a simple manicure.

2016: Victoria Beckham’s square-cut diamond ring

In 2016, Beckham went back to basics with a square-cut diamond ring. She showed off the sparkler at the Cannes Film Festival as she walked the red carpet for the Cafe Society premiere. Wearing a black and white jumpsuit, she paired the ring with two shiny bracelets.

2018: Victoria Beckham’s square-cut yellow diamond ring

The latest addition to Beckham's ring collection is a square-cut yellow diamond ring. She first started wearing the ring in 2018, including various appearances in New York City and Paris Fashion Week. Similar to her other square-cut diamond ring, this one features a pave diamond band.
