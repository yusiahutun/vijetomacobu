How Old Is Kevin Costner? 5 Most-Asked Questions About the Hollywood Legend

Wondering how old Kevin Costner is? Here are five other facts you should know about the actor, including kids’ names and how much he is worth in 2022.

Thirty years after it first hit the screens, “The Bodyguard,” which represents one of Kevin Costner’s most-iconic projects, is returning in a unique partnership with Warner Bros.

The 1992 drama, starring Whitney Houston and Kevin Costner as love interests grossed $411 million worldwide at the time, making it the second highest-grossing film that year.

Despite its success, the movie was critically panned, earning it seven Golden Raspberry nominations including the Worst Actor Award and the Worst Picture Award.

Decades later, the movie is set to grace the theatres again in a whole new dimension, on November 6th and November 9th. In honor of the revival to mark the film’s 30th anniversary, here are five facts you need to know about the movie’s male lead, Kevin Costner.

How Old Is Kevin Costner?

It is not uncommon to find several people asking, “how old is Kevin Costner,” especially as a movie of him from 30 years back is set to grace the screens.

However, the actor may not be as old as you might think. Born on January 18, 1955, Kevin Costner is 67 years of age as of 2022.

His parents, Sharon Rae (mother) and William Costner, (father) welcomed him in Lynwood, California. However, due to his dad’s job as an electrician, Costner kept moving around while growing up, residing in various parts of the state.

What Is Kevin Costner Known For?

Costner kicked off his career as an actor in the mid-seventies, but only found fame nearly a decade later. He is best known for roles in “The Untouchables,” “The Bodyguard,” and the TV show “Yellowstone,” in which he currently stars.

However, he has dabbled into much more than acting career-wise. In the last four decades, Costner has done bits of modeling, singing, and directing. He also produced several movies and runs a successful water treatment company.

How Tall Is Kevin Costner?

At 67, Kevin Costner stands at a height of 6 feet, 1 inch. That’s a far cry from his reported height of 5’ 2” at the time of graduating college. Now that’s some growth spurt.

Notably, he weighs approximately 174 pounds or 79 kg. With his sturdy build, one can hardly decipher how old Kevin Costner is at a glance. His impressive physique has contributed to landing him specific roles throughout his career.

What Is Kevin Costner’s Net Worth?

According to CelebrityNetworth.com, Kevin Costner has a net worth of $250 million as of September 2022.

His money-making ventures include an acting career, music pursuit, movie production and directing, and earnings from his real estate. He has also made a substantial amount from his company, and previous investments in sports and casinos.

Who Are Kevin Costner’s Wife And Kids?

Kevin Costner has been married twice in his lifetime and has seven children in total. His first marriage was to his college sweetheart Cindy Costner in 1978, which lasted until 1994.

Their sixteen years union yielded three children including daughters Annie and Lily, and son Joe Costner. He welcomed another son, Liam Timothy in 1996, with Bridget Rooney, two years after his divorce.

In 2004, “The Postman” star tied the knot again with model Christine Baumgartner, after four years of dating. They share three children, Cayden and Hayes, and Grace.

He values his family over everything else. Earlier in his fatherhood journey, Costner took a four years hiatus from acting to focus on family and his kids’ upbringing.

So how old was Kevin Costner when he became active in the entertainment industry? The exact details about his age at that time is unclear.

However, seeing as his earliest project could be traced to the mid-seventies, one can safely deduce the legend began his showbiz journey in his early twenties. That’s a long time at his disposal to make memories along with his impressive strides.
