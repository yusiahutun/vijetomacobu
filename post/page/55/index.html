Nashville is considered a 10-year-town, so rising country star Jameson Rodgers is hitting his stride right on time. Rodgers — a songwriter-turned-songwriter/artist — has had plenty of bumps in his musical journey, but it was his breaks, including cuts with country powerhouses Florida Georgia Line and a life-changing Instagram message from Luke Combs (more on that later) that catapulted him from Nashville circles to the country music mainstream.
