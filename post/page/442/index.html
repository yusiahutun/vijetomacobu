Qobuz Brings 24-Bit Hi-Res Music Streaming to Sonos Speakers

published on February 26, 2024
Qobuz, the French music streaming service, can now stream 24-bit hi-res music to Sonos speakers. Qobuz is the world's first streaming service to support 24-bit hi-res FLAC music streaming on Sonos speakers. While Tidal also supports hi-res music streaming on Sonos speakers, it does so in 16-bit/44.1 kHz. Sonos speakers can already playback 24-bit FLAC music files locally.
