The Promised Neverland voice actors

The Promised Neverland anime is all about showing the world is a far darker place than the characters ever imagined growing up. The creators have done a fantastic job at making those horrors literal.

Although the animations and graphics contribute majorly to the show's outcome, the characters' voices complete the desired effect. And it is upon The Promised Neverland voice actors to bring out a significant impact.

Emma – Erica Mendez

Erica Mendez is an American voice artist that works for Bang Zoom! Entertainment and FUNimation Entertainment. She is renowned for voicing Ryuko Matoi in Kill la Kill, Akko Kagari in Little Witch Academia, Aladdin in Magi: The Labyrinth of Magic, Diane in Seven Deadly Sins, and many more. Erica voices Emma in The Promised Neverland.

The talented voice actress was born on February 13, 1988, in Chicago, Illinois, United States of America. Her first significant role in the entertainment industry was in the Pac-Man and Ghostly Adventures video game released in 2013.

Isabella – Laura Post

Laura Post plays Isabella from The Promised Neverland. She is an American voice actress and voice director popular for her various works in anime dubbing. Other than her dubbing role for The Promised Neverland, she is renowned for portraying Aria Lieze in Magical Girl Lyrical Nanoha A’s, Ragyo Kiryuin in Kill la Kill, Rosalia in Sword Art Online, Eri Watabe in Lagrange: The Flower of Rin-ne, and many more.

The talented voice artist was born on June 23, 1983, in Chicago, Illinois, USA. She graduated from Columbia College in Chicago in 2007 and was taught by various voicer actors like Steve Staley, Tony Oliver, Huck Ligget, Bob Bergen, Bill Holmes, and Richard Horvitz.
