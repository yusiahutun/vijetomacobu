Former Thrones star Jason Momoa goes barefoot as he arrives to screening of his new film

He is best known for his role as powerful warlord and feared warrior Khal Drogo in Game Of Thrones.

But Jason Momoa decided to ditch the primitive footwear in favour of a much more natural look.

The 34-year-old actor went barefoot as he arrived at the An Evening with Jason Momoa and Thelma Adams event in New York on Tuesday.

Free spirit: Jason Momoa, 34, went barefoot while arriving at An Evening with Jason Momoa and Thelma Adams in New York on Tuesday, which also included a screening of his directorial debut Road To Paloma

Signature role: Jason (pictured right) is best known for his role as powerful warlord and feared warrior Khal Drogo in the HBO series Game Of Thrones as he donned quite primitive footwear on the show

The gala was held at 92nd Street Y and even included a special screening of his film Road To Paloma, which he makes his directorial debut in.

Jason introduced the film at the event, which he co-wrote, directed and stars in with his 46-year-old wife Lisa Bonet.

The former Game Of Thrones star went casual on the outing as he donned a denim blue shirt with the long sleeves rolled up over a white shirt and a pair of worn-down khaki trousers.

Casual customer: The actor and first-time director wore a denim shirt with the sleeves rolled up over a white shirt and worn-down khaki trousers

Hamming it up! Jason seemed to have a good time posing for the cameras as he donned a large brown suede fedora over his long brown locks

He covered up his long brown locks with a large brown suede fedora and his beard appeared to be as scruffy as ever.

The Conan The Barbarian actor accessorised the look with several rings on his fingers as he carried a red handkerchief during the shoeless outing.

Jason even hammed it up for the cameras as he did several different poses on the green carpet including one where he put his two fists forward to show off his rings and another where he did the metal sign of the horns with both hands.

Is that you Drogo? The actor showed off his rings by posing with his two fists extended

According to a report last month from HitFix The Red Road star will be playing Aquaman in upcoming summer blockbuster flick Batman V Superman: Dawn Of Justice.

When not battling villains in a green and orange jumpsuit, the aquatic superhero goes by Arthur Curry.

'[Director] Zack Snyder has already finalized his design for the character so shooting can take place soon,' the site claimed.

Warner Bros has yet to confirm the casting, but if 34-year-old Momoa does get the part, he will join Henry Cavill as Superman, Ben Affleck as Batman, Amy Adams as Lois Lane, Gal Gadot as Wonder Woman, Diane Lane as Martha Kent and Jesse Eisenberg as Lex Luthor.

Directorial debut: Jason co-wrote, directed, and starred in Road To Paloma which centres around a Native American on the run after his mother's murder

There has been so much talk of Jason joining the film that for a time being the actor told reporters to stop asking him about it.

While HitFix noted that Jason's Aquaman will not have a prominent role in the film, the site did point out the character's appearance will further set up the Justice League movie, which will reportedly roll out in May 2017.

Snyder's Batman V Superman: Dawn of Justice is set to hit theaters in 2016.
