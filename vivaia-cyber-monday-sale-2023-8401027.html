Best Vivaia Cyber Monday Shoe Deals:

Regina Pro Heeled Boot

If I had to choose my favorite Vivaia shoe, it would be the Regina Pro. The Regina Pro heeled boots feature a sophisticated square toe, comfortable upper, rubber outsole, and block heel for added stability. But the water-repellant material has got to be my favorite quality. I wear my own pair through puddles, rain storms, and nice weather alike. I never have to sacrifice style because of the outdoors, allowing me to look and feel my best. Not to mention, they come in tons of design options. I gifted my mom a black pair accented by a red heel, though I kept the olive green hue for myself. The next color on my list? The dark caramel hue. 

Julie Pro Chunky Heels

The shoe that started it all — well, for me at least. I still remember snagging the Julie Pro heels towards the beginning of the brand’s history. TBH, I didn’t expect much, though I was surprisingly impressed. The pressure-relief insoles, arch support, round toe, and modest heel sealed the deal; I wore the ultra-comfy heels for 12-plus hours without zero aches or pains. They’re not only comfy, but they’re also versatile and can be effortlessly paired with trousers, dresses, skirts, and everything in between. They’ve since become my go-to heels, and I have a feeling you’ll have a similar experience.    

Margot Mary Janes

No matter how comfy a heel may be, they’re just not for everyone. But Vivaia has you covered with its Margot Mary Janes. Not only are they totally on brand with this season’s trends (think Sarah Jessica Parker and Gigi Hadid), but they may just be some of the sweetest shoes I’ve ever seen. I own a pair in navy and the square toe, slight heel, and buckle strap instantly pulled me in. But the comfort is what made me swoon. The shoe encases my foot while the footbed provides a cloud-like sole. You’ll just have to try them for yourself to know exactly what I’m talking about.
