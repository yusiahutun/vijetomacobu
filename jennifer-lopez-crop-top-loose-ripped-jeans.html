Jennifer Lopez Goes Shopping in a Tiny Crop Top and Ripped Jeans

Another day, another stylish Jennifer Lopez outing.

The superstar was spotted shopping for furniture in Los Angeles yesterday, looking cool in a laid-back outfit composed of a teeny gray scoop-neck crop top, loose ripped wide-leg blue jeans, and white-and-gray sneakers. She accessorized the street look simply, with a beige crossbody bag, a gold chain necklace and chain bracelet, a silver rose-shaped ring, and her signature big gold hoop earrings.

Her hair was styled in a sleek top knot and she wore minimal rosy makeup.

BACKGRID

Lopez has been photographed on various furniture shopping outings since purchasing her new home with husband Ben Affleck.

Earlier this month, she stepped out in a breezy red maxi dress with a halter neckline embellished with a silver strap, plus a matching red croc-embossed Hermès Birkin bag with silver hardware. In true J.Lo fashion, she accessorized with a red croc-embossed leather cuff with silver studs that perfectly matched her Birkin. She also wore her signature platforms, aviator sunglasses, and big hoop earrings. The shoes were golden platform espadrilles embellished at the strap with colorful jewels, the sunglasses had a rosy tint on the lenses to match the red dress, and the hoops were silver to go with the rest of the look.

For a separate shopping trip, she looked fabulous in a shirt dress with a geometric white, red, yellow, and blue print. She completed the look with strappy white platform sandals by Gucci, adorned with silver interlocking G studs, as well as an array of chunky gold jewelry. She also carried a red Hermès flap bag.
