The Matriarch of the Ford Family

Martha Firestone Ford is the matriarch of the famous Ford family, known for their iconic automobile business.

Born and Raised in Ohio

Martha was born and raised in Ohio, where she developed a strong work ethic and a passion for philanthropy.

Wife of the Late William Clay Ford

Martha was married to William Clay Ford, the grandson of Henry Ford, the founder of the Ford Motor Company.

Mother to Four Children

Martha and William Clay Ford have four children together: Martha, Sheila, William Clay Ford Jr., and Elizabeth.

Actively Involved in Philanthropy

Martha is known for her active involvement in various philanthropic endeavors, supporting education, healthcare, and the arts.

The Owner of the Detroit Lions

Martha became the owner and chairwoman of the Detroit Lions, a professional football team based in Detroit, Michigan.

Martha played a key role in the development of Ford Field, the state-of-the-art football stadium where the Detroit Lions play their home games.

Martha’s dedication to the Detroit Lions is unwavering, and she is often seen cheering on her team from the owner’s box.

One of the NFL’s Longest-tenured Owners
