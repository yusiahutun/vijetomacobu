Early life

Erin Burnett from CNN was born and raised in Mardela Springs, Maryland, the United States, on July 2, 1976, to Esther Margret and attorney Kenneth King Burnett. She is the youngest in her family. Her sisters are Mara and Laurie. She is of Irish and Scottish ancestry.
