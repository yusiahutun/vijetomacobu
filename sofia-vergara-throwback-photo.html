Whoa, See Sofia Vergara as a 19-Year-Old Mom!

This photo is amazing.

With Halloween just weeks away, Sofia Vergara shared a pretty epic throwback photo for her son Manolo's first time in costume -- back when she was just 19 years old!

With her naturally lighter locks, the "Modern Family" star is seen surrounded by family and friends in Barranquilla, Colombia in 1992.

It's crazy how young Vergara looks in the photo, one of the rare glimpses into her life as a teenage mother.

By this time in her life, Sofia had already appeared in a Latin American Pepsi commercial and was married to her first husband Joe Gonzalez.

Soon after, she ditched the hubby, started working in the entertainment business ... and the rest is history!
