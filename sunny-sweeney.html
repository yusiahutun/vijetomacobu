Sunny Sweeney Releases New Five-Song EP

Today, the smile on Sunny Sweeney's face couldn't be removed no matter how hard you try. The blonde beauty is riding high over the release of her five-song EP. All five tunes are co-written by Sweeney, who took pride in the selection of songs to offer her loyal fans. &quot;I'm so happy to have music out in the universe again,&quot; Sweeney tells Taste of Country.
