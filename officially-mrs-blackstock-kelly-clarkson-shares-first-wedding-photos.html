'Officially Mrs Blackstock': Kelly Clarkson shares first wedding photos

    Kelly Clarkson has tied the knot with her talent manager fiancé Brandon Blackstock. The country singer said 'I do' in Tennessee on Sunday, and has already shared the first photos from her big day.

    "I'm officially Mrs. Blackstock," Kelly, 31, tweeted on Monday, sharing three beautiful pictures from the wedding. "We got married yesterday at Blackberry Farms in TN, the most beautiful place ever!".

    The blushing bride wore a Temperley London 'Jessamine' wedding gown that featured a plunging neckline, lace overlay, long sleeves and a train.Her blonde locks were loosely pulled back, accessorised with a pretty headband, and she held a bouquet of sunflowers, mixed with white blossoms and Bluebonnets, the official flower of Texas, her home state.In the first picture, the newlyweds can be seen standing in front of a rustic bridge, with the groom giving his bride a kiss on the cheek.
