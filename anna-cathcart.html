Anna Cathcart (born June 16, 2003) is a Canadian actress. She became known for starring in season two of Odd Squad (2016–2019) as Agent Olympia. Cathcart also gained wide recognition for portraying Kitty Covey in Netflix's To All the Boys I've Loved Before film series (2018–2021). She is also known for portraying Dizzy Tremaine in Disney Channel's Descendants 2 (2017) and Descendants 3 (2019) and Zoe Valentine in the Brat web-based series of the same name (2019) and Spring Breakaway (2019).
