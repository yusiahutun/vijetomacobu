Go Mobile

3 handy apps to download before you paddle out

Fishbrain

Upload data about your catches and share photos publicly or privately through this fun social networking app. Peruse others’ uploads to see where fish are biting and what bait is working in real time, log your own personal fishing history, and connect with other anglers. Free on iOS (Fishbrain Pro with upgrade options starts at $9.99/month)

Go Paddling

Let this app do all the work for you as it finds the best places to paddle and the ideal locations to launch your SUP. There are more than 25,000 SUP destinations logged in the app, along with important information such as fees for specific locales and more. Free on iOS

Fish Rules

Understanding state and federal regulations before fishing is essential—and Fish Rules makes it easy to check things like which fish species are in season, how many catches you can keep, and size requirements. Free on iOS and Android.
