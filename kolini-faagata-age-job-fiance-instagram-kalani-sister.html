Who Is Kolini Faagata? Her Age, Job, Fiance, Instagram, Sister

Not only did 90 Day Fiancé star Kalani Faagata herself become famous on the show with her partner Asuelu Pulaa, but she also introduced to viewers her younger sister, Kolini Faagata. Fans have been there when at times things got rough for Kalani, Kolini was the one she called.

Now, let us tell you all about Kolini in the rest of the writing.

90 Day Fiance: Meet Kolini Faagata, Kalani Sister

Kolini Faagata was seen all through season 6 of 90 Day Fiancé and seasons 5 and 6 of 90 Day Fiancé: Happily Ever After? as Kalani’s sister. Since that time, the two sisters also have kept up entertaining fans with their banter on 90 Day Fiancé: Pillow Talk.

Despite Kolini playing a supporting role in her sister’s storyline on 90 Day Fiancé viewers have anyway fallen in love with her. They especially loved her for her commentary on the spinoff, 90 Day Fiancé: Pillow Talk.

Kolini, who jokes about sharing the “same brain” as her sister, has shared how sitting in bed talking “s**t” with her sister equals her favorite thing.

Fans also thought that Kolini is the “most normal person” on the show and lauded her for being outspoken and always speaking the truth over the seasons. She especially never held back about how she felt about her brother-in-law, Asuelu, or his family. Yet, some also had a different opinion about her “getting too involved” in her sister’s relationship.

Who Is Kolini Faagata Fiance?

At the time of this writing, Kolini Faagata continued to be engaged to her fiancé Matthew, AKA Matt.

On her social media, Kolini revealed a romantic beachfront proposal in August 2022. By the look of it, Matthew popped the question in likely on the 28th of August. In the pictures shared, teary and yet happiest at the same time, Kolini showed off her beautiful ring, and before that Matthew presented it to her getting down on one knee. A few days later, Kolini once again gushed about her engagement and thanked everyone who helped make it everything she had ever wanted.

Kolini had not already shared her wedding plans as of now. But, fans already have it believing that she is ready for marriage and kids.

Kolini, who considers herself to be the fun aunt, does not already have any children of her own. People have loved to see her be no less than a mother to her nephews though. One time when the kids’ dad Asuelu was away from America, she actually considered herself to be their father.

We don’t know yet how Kolini met Matthew and how the whole thing started. Just until the December 2021 episodes of 90 Day Fiance, Kolini was teasing him as her new man. She had not already introduced him officially to her family. She only told fans that they will “maybe” find out more about him “in due time”.

Fast forward to today, Kolini has started showing Matthew routinely on her social media posts. Yet even now, she did not give away details beyond than his name. Also, because she had never before tagged him, his social media accounts could not be located.

Kolini and her to-be husband have been seen kissing on Kolini’s social media pictures more than once.

Kolini Faagata Age

Because Kolini Faagata was born in 1991, she turned 31 years old in 2023.

Kolini Faagata Job: What Does She Do For A Living?

Kolini on her LinkedIn goes around as an experienced photographer with a demonstrated history of working in the wedding industry. She writes in its description that she is skilled in wedding, portrait, lifestyle, and fashion photography and that she is a strong art and designs professional with a love for social media and marketing.

Since April 2014, Kolini has been working freelance photographer at Kolini Photography.

Previously, between June 2015 and June 2016, Kolini was the contributing photographer at Locale Lifestyle Magazine. And even before that, from August 2012 through October 2014, she was working as the online retail manager at Hair Comes The Bride.

How Much Is Kolini Faagata Net Worth?

Kolini appears to have only had a high school education. Between 2006 and 2010, she studied at Trabuco Hills High School.
