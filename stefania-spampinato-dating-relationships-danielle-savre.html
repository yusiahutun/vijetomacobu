Is Stefania Spampinato Dating Danielle Savre IRL? Plus Her Relationships

ABC’s Station 19 has captivated the viewers ever since it first premiered back in March 2018. Moreover, after Stefania Spampinato’s addition to the cast as OBGYN Carina DeLuca in season 3, and her marriage with Maya Bishop (Danielle Savre) the next season, the series surely has turned more romantic.

But is their romance real? If not, who is Stefania dating IRL? Scroll down to learn all about Stefania’s relationships. 

Is Stefania Spampinato Dating Danielle Savre IRL?

Stefania Spampinato and Danielle Savre shared a deep connection on Station 19. The two first met each other in season 3, and their relationship slowly progressed over time. But in ep 14 season 4, Stefania (Carina) was forced to pack for her trip back to Italy because her American Visa ran out, and Danielle (Maya), naturally, was upset about it.

And this was where their relationship took a new turn.

Over the ep, the two then shared their secrets. Although Carina shared that she didn’t believe in marriage, Maya insisted that moving in together was already a commitment equal to marriage. One thing led to another, and Carina went on to admit that she wanted to have children. Unfortunately, Maya did not.

So, Carina refused Maya’s marriage proposal and made her way to the airport. On her way there, she then spoke to her ex in Italy, which then made her realize that she made a mistake. Thus, she turned around, found Maya, and told her that she wanted to marry her.

The couple then got married on the season’s finale.

Well, their on-screen romance is one thing, but the two even looked closer when they weren’t acting. A fan wrote, So yeah, after watching that IG live of @D_SAVRE and @spampistefania, im just out here wondering…why aren’t you guys dating??? Y’all are obvs attracted to each other????”

But Stefania Spampinato and Danielle Savre never publicly admitted that they were dating IRL.

Also, Danielle didn’t seem like she was interested in women, given that she was dating Andrew Contis (a video analyst for the Chicago Blackhawks hockey team) in 2018. Reportedly, the two dated for over 4 years and were in a long-distance relationship for most of that time.

Stefania Spampinato Relationships

Stefania Spampinato is a private person. She didn’t share who she was dating or who she dated in the past before 2021.

But while we may know nothing about her relationships, we do know what Stefania was looking for in a potential partner. In an interview with her on-screen girlfriend Danielle, the Station 19 star one shared that she would love it if her date cooked for her. 

Also, she then went on to add that, for her to go on a date with someone, she has to feel pretty strongly about them.

Someday, Stefania wants to fall in love at first sight with someone.

Back in early 2019, Stefania was spotted with her co-star Alberto Frezza at an event for Avengers: Endgame (2019), and a few believed that the two were dating. Unfortunately, we do not have enough information to either confirm or debunk the rumor.

For those of you who want to know, Alberto is an Italian actor who was born on May 23, 1989. Before appearing on Station 19 in 2018, he portrayed “Deputy Garrett Sykes” in the series Dead of Summer, and played “Max” in Angel Falls in Love (2012).

He advised anyone who wanted to have a breakthrough in moviemaking to treat the craft as a profession, and not as a side hustle.
