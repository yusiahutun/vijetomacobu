Shop the &quot;Twilight&quot; x ColourPop Makeup Collaboration

"This is what I am," Edward grimaced as the sun reflected off his iridescent skin. "It's like diamonds... you're beautiful," Bella whispered back. "Beautiful?" he balked back. "This is the skin of a killer, Bella."

This is the scene in which Twilight sold every teen girl on the idea of being a vampire. Sure, you'd kill people and drink blood recreationally—but the glow would be unmatched. And now you can make those shimmery delusions a reality thanks to ColourPop's new makeup collection.

The line, which is available to shop on ColourPop.com starting Sunday, January 14, comprises five shimmer-packed products in all the cool-toned, moody colors you'd expect. Of course, the line features body oil, the SOL Shimmering Dry Body Oil to give you that all-over-glow. It also includes the Twilight Eyeshadow Palette, Lux Lip Oil, Super Shock Highlighter, Graphix Ink Liner—learn more about each below.

SOL Shimmering Dry Body Oil

The shining star of this line is this shimmery body oil. It's lightweight and made with finely mild silver glitter to leave you with that gorgeous diamond-like glow. It has an intoxicating coconut scent to help you lure in your prey.

Twilight Eyeshadow Palette

This palette is made with a blend of cool-toned blues, greens, grays, purples, and taupe in a mix of matte, metallic, matte sparkle, and pearlescent glitter finishes. You can mix and match the different colors to create your perfect Forks-inspired look.

Lux Lip Oil

These sheer glosses will give your pout a fun new hue. It's available in three shades: Bells, a vampy blackened red; Team Edward, a deep rich blue with silver shimmer; and Jacob, a sheer black with silver shimmer.

Super Shock Highlighter

Add a icy dose of highlight with these bouncy powders. Add some to the high points of your cheeks or your lids to add shimmer to your look. It's available in two shades: Meadow, an opalescent duo-chrome lavender; and Vampire Skin, an icy white.

Graphix Ink Liner

This is a super-pigmented liquid eye liner that has a flexible yet sharp felt tip for effortless application. It comes in three shades: Vampire, a metallic deep red; Coven, a rich black with sparkling silver shimmer; and Forever, a metallic forest green.
