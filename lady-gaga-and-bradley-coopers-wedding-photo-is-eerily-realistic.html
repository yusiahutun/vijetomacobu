It appears Lady Gaga fans are still shipping the singer with the Hollywood star Bradley Cooper, and now AI has jumped in to make it a reality – digital reality.

Although Lady Gaga and Bradley Cooper have never been married, fans are still hoping their on-screen chemistry has an ounce of truth. Despite them not actually walking down the aisle, one fan asked AI to recreate the special day as if it did happen.

The performance that sparked a conspiracy theory

The winners of the 2019 Oscars are a far distant memory thanks to Lady Gaga and Bradley Cooper’s performance on the night. Their doting duet caught the attention of fans across the globe due to the steamy number.

However, the celebrity later went on to talk to Jimmy Kimmel after her big win to dispel any rumors about the pair dating.
