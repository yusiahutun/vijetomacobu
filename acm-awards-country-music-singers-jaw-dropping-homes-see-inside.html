Inside country music singers' jaw-dropping homes: Carrie Underwood, Miranda Lambert, Dolly Parton +

Country music's biggest singers have properties worth millions of dollars

    Being a huge country music star can mean long stints away on the road and time apart from family, loved ones, and all the creature comforts of your own home.

    Of course, the likes of Miranda Lambert, Dolly Parton, Keith Urban, Lainey Wilson, and more are no doubt treated to five-star luxury on their travels – but with sprawling homes like theirs, we imagine they don't go too long before they return to their respective jaw-dropping properties.

    With millions of records sold between them, worldwide tours, and lucrative endorsement deals, it's no wonder some of country music's best-loved singers are the proud owners of some palatial real estate. Peek inside some of their homes below…

    Miranda Lambert's home

    Miranda Lambert is a Tennessee resident and owns a $3.4 million home near Primm Springs, roughly an hour's drive south of Nashville. The home is the epitome of country chic and comes with two impeccably decorated guest cabins, a boathouse, a horse barn, a lake, 75 acres of manicured land and wooded hills, and a six-bay equipment garage with a caretaker apartment located on the top level.

    The 3,544 square foot main house features a cozy front porch that overlooks the serene surroundings and holds special meaning for Miranda and her husband, Brendan McLoughlin as it is where they married in January 2019.

    Dolly Parton's home

    Dolly Parton has lived in the same home in Brentwood, Nashville, since purchasing it in 1999 for $400,000 and while she may have bought it for a reasonable fee, it's not lacking when it comes to luxe amenities.

    She shares it with her husband, Carl Dean, and according to the , it features a music room, tennis court, swimming pool, chapel, and a farm.

    Carrie Underwood's home

    Carrie Underwood and her husband Mike Fisher have a beautiful home in Tennessee on a whopping 400-acre plot. The couple spent $3 million on the land in 2011 and went on to build their dream home complete with private horse stables, an expansive garden, and its own lake.

    In 2016, Carrie told "My husband, Mike, and I are designing our forever home. My must-haves are a wood-burning fireplace, a porch swing, and rocking chairs."

    Jason Aldean's home

    Jason Aldean and his wife Brittany built their dream home in a rural area outside of Nashville back in 2020 and it has some exquisite features.

    The property includes a restaurant-quality kitchen, a massive swimming pool with a tiki bar, a man cave for Jason's hunting gear, a two-story walk-in closet as well as an in-home spa, a bowling alley, a virtual golf driving range, and more!

    If that wasn't enough, the abode boasts over 20,000 square feet of space, which makes it one of the biggest country stars' homes.

    Keith Urban's home

    Keith Urban and his wife Nicole Kidman own many residences between them, including an idyllic farmhouse in Nicole's native Australia, a penthouse in Sydney, a condo in New York, and a mansion in Los Angeles. However, they spend most of their time at their main family home in Nashville, Tennessee, which they bought in 2008 for $3.47 million.

    Boasting seven bedrooms, eight bathrooms, a tennis court, a swimming pool, a theater, and a home recording studio, the 10,925-square-foot home has plenty of space for Nicole, Keith, and their daughters, Faith and Sunday.

    Lainey Wilson's home

    When Lainey Wilson first moved to Nashville in August 2011, she lived in a camper trailer that she parked in her friend's parking lot for three years. Since then, a lot has changed for the singer and she now has a home she can call her own – she's even building her dream house. 

    Lainey's current property boasts a front porch with an off-white railing, banisters, and window shutters painted in a deep mahogany hue. The simple yet chic inside features an open-plan kitchen, dining, and living area with a nod to her love of the 70s era.

    Kane Brown's home

    Kane Brown dropped $1.6 million on his luxury mansion in Whites Creek, Tennessee — a suburb of Nashville – back in 2016. The 4,515 square feet home sits on 30 acres of land surrounded by verdant forests and rolling hills and features four bedrooms and three bathrooms. 

    The property is a mixture of modern and rustic elements and boasts wooden beams, and concrete and stone accents. It also has a swimming pool, a playroom for his two children, Kodi and Kingsley, and a two-car garage.
