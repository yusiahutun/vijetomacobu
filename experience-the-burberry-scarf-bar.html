Experience the Burberry scarf bar

Pictures courtesy of Burberry

Burberry is celebrating one of its most popular heritage pieces this season; the cashmere scarf.

For the first time ever, customers will be able to fully customise their purchase thanks to the introduction of the new Burberry Scarf Bar - available in stores and online - which will allow them to select colour (out of 30 different shades), print (check, heart, plain) and weight of cashmere (lightweight or classic), plus a different colour thread for monogramming. 

To celebrate the launch of the scarf bar, Burberry has released a short film shot at the Scottish Mills in Elgin and Ayr (which produce all Burberry scarves), demonstrating the expert craftsmanship that goes into each product. Each scarf is created from 100% cashmere, carefully woven on a traditional loom and finished by hand.
