Taylor Swift's unexpected effect on Travis Kelce 'could affect his future'

Experts believe that Taylor Swift’s influence on Travis Kelce could have the best impact on his future especially as it can make him more focused.

Reports suggest that Travis Kelce has had a reputation as the NFL playboy for a while. The American footballer participated in the TV show Catching Kelce in 2016 where he dated one woman from each state in search of his ‘soulmate.’ While Catching Kelce could not ultimately help him find a soulmate, experts believe that Taylor Swift‘s company can help him change his playboy reputation once and for all.

Thanks to Taylor, Travis Kelce can be ‘focused’ now

American sports expert John Salley believes that Taylor can bring the biggest change in Travis Kelce’s career. The American former professional basketball player commented on how Travis can finally be focused and not worry about 100 direct messages as he pursues Taylor.

“He now doesn’t have to worry about 100 DMs,” adding “or making sure certain girls got tickets. He can be focused, right?” Salley also addressed Kelce’s apparent playboy status saying that talking to Taylor will lead him to have a simpler lifestyle. He gushed: “So, he has practice, go to sleep, talk to his girl. That’s easier than when you get to be the playboy that he used to be.”

Friends of Travis fear heartbreak for Taylor

Taylor Swift has been spotted in not one but two games of Travis Kelce’s Kansas City Chiefs game and the second game saw a number of Hollywood A-listers celebrities including Blake Lively, Ryan Reynolds, and Hugh Jackman. While Swifties believe that Taylor’s friends attended the game as an approval of the celebrity couple, friends of the footballer are rather skeptical about the new blooming connection.

Taylor and Travis have not made their relationship official and an insider reportedly says that Taylor might end up being ‘sacked and single’ before long! “Travis has charmed her for now, but they’re afraid she’ll be sacked and single before long,” the insider says.
