Who is Singer Jonny West? His Age, Songs &amp; More

Who is American Idol Season 18 Contestant Jonny West?

Jonny was one of the talented contenders on American Idol season 18. The American singer-songwriter sang his original song, Makin Love, at the audition, and the judges selected him to go the Hollywood round. Jonny was in the Singer-songwriter category in the Genre challenge. Jonny studied songwriting in music school. The audience and judges loved his lyrics as much as his voice and showmanship. 

Jonny has over one hundred thousand followers on his Instagram account. He provides the latest updates on his music and life through the platform. The young artist uploads his original music on YouTube. More than twenty thousand people subscribe to his channel. 

Biography Timeline

Jonny West is an American singer-songwriter who gained fame after his impressive American Idol Season 18 performances. Born on October 15, 1996, in Murrieta, California, West grew up in a musical family and started playing the guitar at a young age. He honed his skills and pursued his passion for music, ultimately leading him to the American Idol stage.

  • October 15, 1996: Jonny West is born in Murrieta, California.
  • 2002: West starts playing the guitar at the age of 6.
  • 2018: West graduates from California Baptist University with a degree in music education.
  • 2019: West auditions for American Idol Season 18 and receives a golden ticket to Hollywood.
  • 2020: West makes it to the Top 20 of American Idol, winning over audiences with his original songs and emotional performances.
  • May 17, 2020: West performs his original song “Makin’ Love” on American Idol and receives praise from the judges.
  • May 19, 2020: West is eliminated from American Idol, finishing in the Top 5.
  • May 22, 2020: West releases his debut EP, “Jonny West EP,” which features four original songs and showcases his unique sound and style.
  • August 2020: West signs with Hollywood Records, a division of Disney Music Group.
  • November 2020: West releases his single “What a Wonderful World,” a classic Louis Armstrong song cover featuring his soulful vocals and heartfelt interpretation.
  • January 2021: West releases his single “Justified,” highlighting his songwriting and music skills.
  • February 2021: West performs on American Idol’s “Comeback” episode and advances to the next round.
  • April 2021: West releases his single “Better Now,” a catchy and upbeat track that showcases his versatility as an artist.
With his heartfelt performances and soulful voice, Jonny West has quickly become a rising star in the music industry. His debut EP and singles have received critical acclaim, and his fan base continues to grow. As he continues to write and record new music, it’s clear that West has a bright future ahead of him in pieces.
