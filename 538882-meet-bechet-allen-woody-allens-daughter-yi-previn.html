Who is Bechet Allen?

Bechet Dumaine Allen, the adopted daughter of film director Woody Allen and Soon-Yi Previn, was born in China on December 21, 1998. She is known for the TV films Rifkin's Festival (2020) and Coup de Chance (2023), where she worked on the costumes.

Bechet Allen attended Bard College, majoring in art history. In addition, she has a young sister Manzie Tio Allen, adopted from Texas.

Bechet Allen parents’ controversy

Soon-Yi Previn, from Korea, was adopted by Hollywood actress Mia Farrow and her ex-husband, Ander Previn, at age six. Woody Allen met Soon-Yi Previn when she was in the 11th grade while dating Mia Farrow. Soon-Yi happened to injure her leg while playing soccer, and Allen offered to take her to school.
