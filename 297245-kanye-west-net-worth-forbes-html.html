Music career

Kanye dropped out of college to concentrate on his music. He started out making beats for up-and-coming local artists in his neighbourhood before settling on a technique that emphasized speeding up vocal samples from classic soul albums.

He received his big break in 2000 when he started producing for Roc-A-Fella musicians. He rose to prominence after contributing to Jay-Z's The Blueprint album in 2001.

He later came to the limelight after an accident that left him with a shattered jaw. It is through this condition that he released his first album, Through the Wire.

This laid a foundation for him as a rapper, and as they say, the rest is history; after his first albums, West has established himself as one of Hip-Hop's most successful and unrivalled talents.

Fashion business

Apart from music, West has also dabbled in the fashion sector. Kanye announced the launch of his Pastelle Clothing line in 2005.

In 2009, West collaborated with Nike to realize his shoe, the Air Yeezys. In addition, he introduced his first shoe line designed for Louis Vuitton during Puris fashion week the same year.

In 2013 Adidas officially confirmed working together with Kanye to realize Adidas Yeezy boots. Although the Adidas Yeezy boots were limited to 9000 pairs available only in New York City, they were sold within 10 minutes.

In May 2021, Kanye signed a 10-year deal linking Yeezy with Gap Inc. The first piece from the collection was realised in June 2021, a blue, seamless, down jacket and it was sold instantly.

Business ventures

With Sony BMG, Kanye formed G.O.O.D Music, a record label and production company, in 2004. The term G.O.O.D stands for "getting out our dreams." Artists signed to the label include Kacy Hill, John Legend, Big Sean, Pusha, Common, T, Q-Tip, and Teyana Taylor.

Kanye West is a co-owner of Tidal's music streaming service, with various artists such as Jay-Z, Rihanna, Beyoncé, Madonna, Chris Martin, and Nicki Minaj. The majority owns a 3% equity stake. The service specializes in audio and high definition music videos.

He also started Yeezy architecture arm called Yeezy home and KW Foods LLC, a Chicago-based company that owns the Fatburger fast-food franchise.

Other endeavours

In July 2020, Kanye announced that he would run in the 2020 presidential election. Former President Donald Trump supported West's candidacy, believing that he would lure some voters away from Democratic nominee Joe Biden.

Kanye chose Michelle Tidball as his running mate, and they had 70,296 votes, 0.04% of the national votes. However, he later implied that he intends to run for president in 2024 due to Donald Trump's win in the 2016 elections.

He has also made a significant contribution to the support of presidential candidates in the United States. West provided $1,000 to Barack Obama's re-election campaign in September 2012 and $2,700 to Hillary Clinton's 2016 campaign in August 2015. His philanthropic endeavours include the Kanye West Foundation, which supports education for African American and Latino youth and donating money to organizations such as Hurricane Katrina assistance, World Water Day, and Live Earth.
