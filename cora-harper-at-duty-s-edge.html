Cora Harper: At Duty's Edge, Mass Effect: Andromeda Mission

1. Read e-mail from Cora

Read the email "The asari ark" from Cora.

Ryder,

Had a breakthrough on the location of the asari ark. Please, come talk to me on the Tempest ASAP.

Cora.

2. Speak to Cora on the Tempest

3. Search the Valay system for Ark Leusinia

Go to the Valay system and search for an anomaly.

4. Dock with Ark Leusinia

5. Reach the bridge

Go to the bridge and talk to Captain Atandra and Sarissa.

6. Ride the tram to Hangar Control

7. Look for asari commandos in Hangar Control

Find and talk to Vederia.

8. Activate hangar door override

9. Defend the hangar door override console

10. Eliminate the remaining kett

11. Speak to Vederia

12. Go below decks through the living quarters

Go through the living quarters. You should check the rooms for containers, kill some kett, etc.

13. Secure Fire Control

Kill the kett.

14. Reactivate the fire suppression system

Try to use the fire suppression console.

14.1. Scan for a way to reroute power to the fire suppression system

15. Meet Vederia

She is waiting for you on the Observation Deck.

16. Scan the mass effect field device

17. Disconnect power cables (x3)

Yuo need to disconnect three cables. Two cables are on the lower level, one more is on the upper level of the Observation Deck.

17.1. Eliminate the kett on the Observation Deck

Every time you disconnect one of them, some kett will attack you. Kill them all.

18. Disable the mass effect field device

You will learn that Sarissa sacrificed the original Pathfinder in battle with the kett.

19. Go to Deck Twelve

20. Repel the kett attack on Deck Twelve

20.1. Optional: Trigger mass effect field

At some point, the Valiant will join the fight.

The asari ark is safe now.

Now you have to decide if Sarissa should still be the Pathfinder or someone else - Vederia, should take her place.

During the conversation with Sarissa: "We should keep this quiet" or "The asari should know".

If you decided that asari should know what happened, then you will have to make another decision: "She'll be fine" or "Keep Sarissa as Pathfinder".

During the conversation with Cora: "Time limited decision": "Hug Cora".

Cora's Rank 6 skills are now unlocked.
