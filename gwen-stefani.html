Gwen Stefani: Latest News &amp; Photos

All the latest from American singer, songwriter, fashion designer and record producer Gwen Stefani. The talented artist rose to fame as the main songwriter of 80s ska band No Doubt. During the band's hiatus in the 2000s, Gwen started a solo career as a pop artist, resulting in the album Love. Angel. Music. Baby. The album was a huge success, featuring hit singles What You Waiting For? and Hollaback Girl. She was previously married to English musician Gavin Rossdale, however the pair parted ways in 2016 after a 14-year marriage. The pair share three sons: Kingston, Apollo and Zuma. Gwen is now engaged to American TV star Blake Shelton, whom she met whilst judging The Voice in 2014. The couple revealed their engagement in October 2020.
