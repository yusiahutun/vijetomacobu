Top Travel Influencers To Follow For Inspiration

When all the world is going back to normal, so does the travel sector. If you are planning to travel but don’t know where, read along for some travel influencers who can give you inspiration for your next destination. We all miss traveling, and it’s time we get those plane tickets!

Top Travel Influencers for Inspiration

1. The Bucket List Family

The Bucket List Family is the true bucket list for families around the world. Garret and Jess, aka the dad and mom, share their experiences of traveling with kids while still enjoying what the destinations have to offer. If you are looking for a cosy travel account that can inspire you, make sure to check them out.

2. Chris Burkard

If you love nature and photography, you have to follow Chris Burkard. The famous photographer shares some incredible shots from all around the world, and it is enough to make you grab your suitcase and passport to see llamas in California.

3. Louis Cole

The vlogger and traveller Louis shares his journeys from the very start to the end. He is also an advocate for social issues such as protecting nature while traveling the world, and much more. Follow him if you want someone that feels familiar.

4. Aggie

Aggie, also known as Travel in Her Shoes, is one of the oldest travel accounts on Instagram. Her page is very colorful, and she seems to be having fun all the time. If you are in need of somewhere that has entertainment, check out her profile. You’ll find more than what you are looking for. 

5. Christian LeBlanc

Once a 9-5 worker, Christian quit his job to pursue his traveling passion. He is now also helping other creators to create their own brand, and be successful at what they love doing. 

6. The Planet D

Dave & Deb are one of the most influential couples on Instagram, sharing their travel stories not only in the form of IG posts but also as blog articles. Even though we are in the age of fast consumption, they are still writing about places they go, and it is truly amazing to have so much information about a destination without even traveling there.

Bonus:

Johnny Ward

One of the escapees of 9-5 works, Johnny is the first person to travel all the countries in the world. His latest adventure, rowing through the Atlantic Ocean is something you don’t want to miss. Click here to hear his answers to our questions, and go follow him for some more crazy adventures.
