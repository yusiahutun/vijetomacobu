The Best Vegan Makeup and Skincare for Women Over 50

As a nation, we’ve become more conscious of what we put in — and on — our bodies. We’re reading labels on packages of pastas and shampoos, and our browser tabs are filled with Google searches like what is glycerin and how many trans fats are in french fries. And that’s a good thing!

Read More

When it comes to beauty products, everyone has their own criteria about what’s healthiest to put on their skin, hair, and nails. For some, clean beauty products (free of toxins) are preferred, while others opt for natural makeup and skincare (sourced from nature).

But lately, it’s vegan makeup that’s creating all the buzz in the beauty world. What makes vegan makeup vegan, you might ask? Vegan products don’t contain animal by-products, and they omit toxic chemicals like heavy metals, sodium lauryl sulfate, polyethylene, petroleum distillates, and parabens. (Believe it or not, those are common ingredients in many beauty products!)
