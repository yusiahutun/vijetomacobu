What is Pitbulls and Parolees show about?

The TV show centres on the Villalobos Rescue centre, which rehabilitates numerous aggressive pit bulldogs. Moreover, it incorporates former inmates who can retrain the dogs while also preparing them to be assimilated back to society. The centre was established by Tia Torres, a top pitbull trainer. It is one of the largest pit bull rescue centres in the world.
