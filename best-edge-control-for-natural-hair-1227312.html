    How to Choose the Best Edge Control for Natural Hair?

    Here are some points to see before buying the best edge control for natural hair. 

    Ingredients 

    Activate your eagle eyes and scan through the ingredients. Go for edge controls with natural, hydrating and moisturizing agents like essential oils, glycerin, plant extracts, etc. Ditch options with alcohol, sulfates, parabens and other harsh chemicals. 

    Safety 

    Choose products that are gentle on the skin and scalp. Stay away from irritants or allergens. You can select one with hypoallergenic raw materials. 

    Formula 

    Edge controllers are available in different formats like gels and wax. Gels are typically water-based and waxes are oil-based. Water-based formulas work best on loose curls and straight hair, whereas, oil-based ones help manage dull, dry and coily manes.

    FAQ's

    Can edge control curl hair?

    Edge controls work differently on different types of hair. You can check out specific products that align with haircare needs.

    Is Edge Control better than gel?

    Edge controls generally have a thicker consistency. They are best to consider when it comes to manipulating your frizzy hair.

    Can edge control damage your edges?

    Edge controls can negatively impact edges if you're using them too frequently and excessively.

    Can I sleep with edges?

    You can wrap your edges with a scarf or bonnet to protect them from damage due to friction while sleeping.
